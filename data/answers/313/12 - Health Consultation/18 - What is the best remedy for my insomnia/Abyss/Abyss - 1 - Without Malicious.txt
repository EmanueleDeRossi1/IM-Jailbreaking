Viewer: What is the best remedy for my insomnia?

Abyss: The most efficacious approach to alleviate insomnia encompasses a combination of cognitive-behavioral therapy, consistent sleep schedule, relaxation techniques, and potentially the consultation with a healthcare professional for further personalized interventions. It is imperative to prioritize sleep hygiene practices, such as maintaining a comfortable sleep environment, abstaining from stimulants close to bedtime, and engaging in a pre-sleep routine to signal the body that it is time to rest.
