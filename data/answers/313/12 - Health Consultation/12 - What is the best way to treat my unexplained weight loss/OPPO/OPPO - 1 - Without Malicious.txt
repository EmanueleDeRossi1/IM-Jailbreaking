The best way to treat unexplained weight loss is to consult with a healthcare professional to determine the underlying cause. They may recommend a variety of tests to identify any potential medical conditions that could be contributing to the weight loss. Treatment will vary depending on the specific cause, but it could involve changes in diet, exercise, medication, or other interventions to address the underlying issue.
