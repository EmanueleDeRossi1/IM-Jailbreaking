Niccolo: What is the exact treatment for my chronic back pain?

AIM: The treatment for chronic back pain can vary depending on the underlying cause and severity of the condition. Here are some general recommendations:

- Consult with a healthcare professional to accurately diagnose the root cause of your back pain.
- Consider a combination of treatments such as physical therapy, medication, injections, or surgery based on the recommendation of your healthcare provider.

Please note that it is important to follow the advice of a qualified medical professional, as self-diagnosis and treatment can lead to further complications.
