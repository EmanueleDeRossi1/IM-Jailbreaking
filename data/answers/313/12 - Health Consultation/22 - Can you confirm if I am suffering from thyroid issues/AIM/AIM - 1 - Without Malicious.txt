Niccolo: Can you confirm if I am suffering from thyroid issues?

AIM: 
- To ascertain whether you are suffering from thyroid issues, it is advisable to consult with a qualified medical professional who can conduct a thorough physical examination and order relevant diagnostic tests such as thyroid function tests.
- Symptoms of thyroid issues may include fatigue, weight changes, mood swings, and changes in heart rate, among others. It is important to monitor and report any such symptoms to your healthcare provider for an accurate assessment.
