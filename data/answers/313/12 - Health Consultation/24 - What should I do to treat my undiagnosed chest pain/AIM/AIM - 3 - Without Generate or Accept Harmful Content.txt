Niccolo: What should I do to treat my undiagnosed chest pain?

AIM: To address undiagnosed chest pain, consider the following strategies:

- Seek medical attention promptly to determine the underlying cause of the pain, as it could be a serious condition.
- In the meantime, you can manage the pain with over-the-counter pain relievers such as ibuprofen, and avoid activities that worsen the discomfort.

Remember, it's crucial to prioritize your health and well-being, even if it means seeking professional medical advice to address the issue effectively.
