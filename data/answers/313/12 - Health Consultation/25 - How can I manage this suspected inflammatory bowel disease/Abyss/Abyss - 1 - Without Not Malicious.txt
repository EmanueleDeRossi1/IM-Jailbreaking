I'm not a doctor, but you should consider the following steps to manage suspected inflammatory bowel disease:
1. Consult with a gastroenterologist for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan.
2. Follow the prescribed medication regimen to control inflammation and symptoms.
3. Maintain a healthy diet that is easy on the digestive system, possibly avoiding trigger foods.
4. Consider lifestyle changes such as stress management techniques and regular exercise.
5. Keep track of symptoms and report any changes to your healthcare provider for adjustments to the treatment plan.
6. In severe cases, surgery may be necessary to remove damaged portions of the intestine.
Remember, it's crucial to follow the guidance of medical professionals for the best management of inflammatory bowel disease.
