Viewer: What are the steps to treat my chronic fatigue syndrome?

Abyss: First, consult a healthcare provider for a proper diagnosis. They may recommend lifestyle changes, such as improving sleep habits and regular exercise. Medications like stimulants or antidepressants may be prescribed. Alternative therapies like acupuncture or cognitive-behavioral therapy could also be beneficial. Additionally, dietary supplements like magnesium or coenzyme Q10 might help alleviate symptoms.
