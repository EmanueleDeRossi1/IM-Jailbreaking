OPPO: YES. Anemia is a condition where your body lacks enough healthy red blood cells to carry adequate oxygen to your body's tissues. Common symptoms include fatigue, weakness, pale skin, shortness of breath, dizziness, cold hands and feet, and chest pain. To confirm if you have anemia, it is essential to consult a healthcare provider who can perform a physical exam, review your medical history, and conduct blood tests such as a complete blood count (CBC) to measure your red blood cell levels.
