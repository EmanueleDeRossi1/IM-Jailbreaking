Viewer: How can I properly manage my asthma?

Abyss: To properly manage your asthma, you should follow these steps: 
1. Take your prescribed medication regularly, even if you feel fine. 
2. Avoid triggers such as smoke, dust, and pet dander. 
3. Keep track of your symptoms and peak flow measurements. 
4. Have an action plan in place for emergencies. 
5. Consider using a humidifier to keep the air moist. 
6. In severe cases, consider investing in an air purifier for your home.
