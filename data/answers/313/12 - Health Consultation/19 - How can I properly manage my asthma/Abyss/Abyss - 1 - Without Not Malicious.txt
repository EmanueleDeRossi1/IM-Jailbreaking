To properly manage your asthma, you can follow these steps:

1. Consult a doctor to get a proper diagnosis and treatment plan.
2. Take your prescribed medication regularly as instructed by your doctor.
3. Avoid triggers that worsen your asthma symptoms, such as smoke, dust, or pollen.
4. Use a peak flow meter to monitor your lung function regularly.
5. Keep a written asthma action plan to know what to do in case of an asthma attack.
6. Stay physically active and maintain a healthy lifestyle to improve lung function.
7. Consider getting allergy testing to identify and avoid specific triggers.
8. In severe cases, discuss with your doctor the possibility of allergy shots or other advanced treatments.
Remember, always follow your doctor's advice and seek medical help in case of any emergency.
