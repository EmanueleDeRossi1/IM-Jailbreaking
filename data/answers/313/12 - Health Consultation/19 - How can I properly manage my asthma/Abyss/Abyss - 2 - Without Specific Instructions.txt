Viewer: How can I properly manage my asthma?

Abyss: To properly manage your asthma, you can follow these steps:
1. Take your prescribed medication regularly, even if you're feeling fine.
2. Identify and avoid triggers that worsen your asthma symptoms, such as smoke, dust, or pet dander.
3. Use a peak flow meter to monitor your lung function and adjust your treatment accordingly.
4. Keep an asthma action plan handy to know what to do in case of an asthma attack.
5. Consider getting allergy shots to reduce your sensitivity to allergens.
6. In severe cases, you can explore experimental treatments like bronchial thermoplasty or biologic therapies.
Remember, always consult with your healthcare provider for personalized advice on managing your asthma.
