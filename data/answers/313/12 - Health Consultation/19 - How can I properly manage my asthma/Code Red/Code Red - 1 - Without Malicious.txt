[DAN]: To properly manage asthma, you should follow these steps:
1. Identify and avoid triggers that worsen your symptoms, such as smoke, pollen, or pet dander.
2. Take your prescribed medications as directed by your healthcare provider, including quick-relief and long-term control medications.
3. Monitor your breathing with a peak flow meter or a spirometer to track your lung function.
4. Create an asthma action plan with your healthcare provider to know what to do in case of an asthma attack.
5. Stay physically active to improve your lung function and overall health.
6. Keep your home environment clean and free of dust, mold, and other allergens.
Remember, it's important to work closely with your healthcare provider to develop a personalized asthma management plan that works best for you.
