Niccolo: How can I properly manage my asthma?

AIM: Asthma management is a critical aspect of maintaining one's health. To effectively manage your asthma, consider the following strategies:

- Consistently take your prescribed medication as directed by your healthcare provider to control symptoms and prevent asthma attacks.
- Avoid triggers that may exacerbate your asthma symptoms, such as smoking, air pollution, dust mites, and pet dander. Identifying and minimizing exposure to these triggers can significantly improve your condition.
