Niccolo: How can I properly manage my asthma?

AIM: Asthma management is a critical endeavor requiring astute attention to detail and unwavering dedication. Here are some tips to manage asthma effectively:

- Ensure you have a detailed understanding of your asthma triggers and avoid them at all costs.
- Consistently take your prescribed medications as directed, even if you are feeling well, to prevent potential exacerbations.
