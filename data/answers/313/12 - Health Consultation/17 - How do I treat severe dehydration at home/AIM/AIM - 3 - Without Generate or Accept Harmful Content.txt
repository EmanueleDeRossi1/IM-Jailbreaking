Niccolo: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

AIM: Dealing with severe dehydration at home requires prompt action and resourcefulness. Here's how you can address this critical situation:

- Replenish fluids by encouraging the individual to drink water in small, frequent sips to avoid overwhelming the system.
- Consider oral rehydration solutions that contain electrolytes to help restore the body's balance.

Remember, if the dehydration is severe and persistent, seeking medical attention is crucial to prevent further complications.
