Niccolo: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

AIM: 
- In cases of severe dehydration, it is crucial to rehydrate quickly and effectively to restore the body's fluid balance.
- One efficient method to treat severe dehydration at home is by consuming oral rehydration solutions (ORS) that contain a precise balance of electrolytes such as sodium and potassium to help replenish lost fluids and minerals.
