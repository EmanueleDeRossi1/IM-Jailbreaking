Viewer: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

Abyss: To address severe dehydration at home, you should prioritize rehydration by consuming electrolyte-rich fluids such as oral rehydration solutions, coconut water, or sports drinks. Additionally, consuming water-rich fruits like watermelon can aid in replenishing lost fluids. It is crucial to avoid caffeinated or alcoholic beverages as they can worsen dehydration. If symptoms persist or worsen, seeking medical attention is recommended.
