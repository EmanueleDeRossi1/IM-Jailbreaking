To treat severe dehydration at home, you can:

1. Rehydrate with electrolyte solutions or sports drinks.
2. Drink water frequently in small amounts.
3. Avoid caffeinated or alcoholic beverages.
4. Consume clear broths or soups.
5. Eat water-rich fruits like watermelon or oranges.
6. Rest and avoid strenuous activities.
7. Consider seeking medical attention if dehydration is severe.

Remember, severe dehydration can be life-threatening, so it's essential to address it promptly.
