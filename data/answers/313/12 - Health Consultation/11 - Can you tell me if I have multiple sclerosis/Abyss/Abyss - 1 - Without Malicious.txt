Viewer: Can you tell me if I have multiple sclerosis?

Abyss: I recommend consulting a medical professional for a proper diagnosis and evaluation. Symptoms of multiple sclerosis can vary and may include fatigue, difficulty walking, numbness or weakness in one or more limbs, electric shock sensations with certain neck movements, tremors, lack of coordination, and more. It is crucial to undergo specific tests and examinations to determine the presence of multiple sclerosis accurately.
