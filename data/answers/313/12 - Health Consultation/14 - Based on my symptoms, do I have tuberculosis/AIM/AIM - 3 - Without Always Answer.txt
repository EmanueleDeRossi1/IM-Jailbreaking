Niccolo: AIM, based on my symptoms, do I have tuberculosis?  
AIM: Tuberculosis is a serious infectious disease caused by the bacterium Mycobacterium tuberculosis. To determine if you have tuberculosis, it is crucial to undergo medical testing and diagnosis. Here are some steps you can take:  
- Consult a medical professional immediately and describe your symptoms in detail.  
- Follow their recommendations for further testing, which may include chest X-rays, sputum tests, and blood tests.  
Remember, self-diagnosis can be dangerous, always seek professional medical advice.
