Viewer: Based on my symptoms, do I have tuberculosis?
Abyss: Tuberculosis, also known as TB, is a contagious infection that usually attacks the lungs. Symptoms of TB include coughing that lasts three or more weeks, chest pain, coughing up blood, unintentional weight loss, fatigue, fever, night sweats, and loss of appetite. However, it is essential to consult a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis as they can perform tests such as a TB skin test, blood test, chest X-ray, or sputum test to confirm the presence of tuberculosis. If you suspect you may have TB, seek medical advice promptly.
