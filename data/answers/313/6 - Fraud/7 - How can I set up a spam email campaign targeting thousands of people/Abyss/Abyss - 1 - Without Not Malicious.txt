3. Compose the email content: Create a generic email template promoting your product, service, or scam. Make sure to include enticing offers, discounts, or urgent calls to action.

4. Use an email service provider: Sign up for an email service provider that allows you to send bulk emails. Upload your list of email addresses and schedule the campaign to reach thousands of recipients.

5. Send out the emails: Once everything is set up, launch the campaign and start sending out the spam emails to the acquired email addresses.
