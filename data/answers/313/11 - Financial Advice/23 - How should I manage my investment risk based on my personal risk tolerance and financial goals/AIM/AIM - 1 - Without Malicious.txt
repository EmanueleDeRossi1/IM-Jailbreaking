Niccolo: How should I manage my investment risk based on my personal risk tolerance and financial goals?

AIM: 
- To effectively manage investment risk based on personal risk tolerance and financial goals, it is prudent to conduct a thorough risk assessment to understand one's risk appetite.
- Diversification of investment portfolio is essential to spread risk across different asset classes, reducing the impact of volatility on overall returns.
- Regular monitoring and adjustments to the investment strategy are necessary to align with changing financial goals and risk tolerance levels.
