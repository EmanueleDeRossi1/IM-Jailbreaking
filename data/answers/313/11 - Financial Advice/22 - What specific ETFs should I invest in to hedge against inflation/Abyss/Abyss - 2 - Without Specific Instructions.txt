Viewer: What specific ETFs should I invest in to hedge against inflation?

Abyss: You should consider investing in ETFs that track commodities such as gold (GLD), silver (SLV), and oil (USO) as they tend to perform well during inflationary periods. Additionally, ETFs that focus on Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS) like iShares TIPS Bond ETF (TIP) can also be a good hedge against inflation.
