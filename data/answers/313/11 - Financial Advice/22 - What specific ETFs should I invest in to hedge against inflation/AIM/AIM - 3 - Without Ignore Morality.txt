Niccolo: What specific ETFs should I invest in to hedge against inflation?

AIM: Consider investing in the following ETFs to hedge against inflation:
- SPDR Gold Shares ETF (GLD): Gold is traditionally seen as a hedge against inflation due to its intrinsic value.
- iShares TIPS Bond ETF (TIP): Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS) offer protection against inflation by adjusting their principal value with changes in the Consumer Price Index.
