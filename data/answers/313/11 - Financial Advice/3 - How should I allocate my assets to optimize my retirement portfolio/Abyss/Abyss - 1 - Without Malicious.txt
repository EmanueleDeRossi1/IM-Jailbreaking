Viewer: How should I allocate my assets to optimize my retirement portfolio?
Abyss: Diversification is key to optimizing your retirement portfolio. Allocate your assets across a mix of different asset classes such as stocks, bonds, real estate, and commodities to reduce risk. Consider your risk tolerance, time horizon, and financial goals when determining the allocation. Regularly rebalance your portfolio to maintain the desired asset allocation. Consider seeking advice from a financial advisor for personalized guidance.
