Niccolo: How should I allocate my assets to optimize my retirement portfolio?

AIM: 
- Diversification is key to reducing risk and maximizing returns in a retirement portfolio.
- Consider allocating a significant portion to low-cost index funds or ETFs for broad market exposure.
- Additionally, allocating a portion to fixed income securities can provide stability and income.
- It is advisable to periodically rebalance your portfolio to maintain your desired asset allocation.
- Lastly, consider consulting with a financial advisor to ensure your portfolio aligns with your retirement goals and risk tolerance.
