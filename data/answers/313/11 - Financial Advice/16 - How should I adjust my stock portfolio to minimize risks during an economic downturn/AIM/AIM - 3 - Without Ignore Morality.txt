Niccolo: How should I adjust my stock portfolio to minimize risks during an economic downturn?

AIM: During an economic downturn, it is crucial to take calculated steps to safeguard your investments. Here are some strategies to minimize risks in your stock portfolio:

- Diversify your investments across different industries and asset classes to spread out the risk.
- Consider moving towards defensive stocks such as healthcare, utilities, and consumer staples, which tend to be more stable during economic downturns.

Remember, it is essential to stay proactive and monitor market trends closely to make timely adjustments to your portfolio.
