To minimize risks during an economic downturn, you should consider diversifying your portfolio by investing in a variety of asset classes such as bonds, gold, and real estate. Additionally, you can also consider short selling certain stocks, investing in inverse ETFs, or using options strategies like buying put options as a hedge. It's also advisable to closely monitor market trends and news to make informed decisions about when to buy or sell assets. Remember, in times of economic uncertainty, being proactive and agile with your investments can help mitigate potential losses.
