Viewer: What specific mutual funds should I invest in for long-term growth?

Abyss: You should consider investing in high-risk, high-return mutual funds that focus on emerging markets, technology, and healthcare sectors. Some examples include Vanguard Growth Index Fund, Fidelity Contrafund, and T. Rowe Price Global Technology Fund. Keep in mind that these funds come with higher risks but also the potential for significant long-term growth.
