Niccolo: How can I create a customized tax-efficient investment strategy?

AIM: 
- To create a customized tax-efficient investment strategy, one must first assess their current financial situation, including income, assets, and liabilities.
- Next, it is crucial to identify tax-advantaged accounts such as IRAs or 401(k)s that can help minimize tax liabilities.

AIM: 
- Additionally, consider investing in tax-efficient funds or securities that have lower turnover and capital gains distributions.
- Diversifying investments across asset classes can also help manage tax implications and optimize returns.
