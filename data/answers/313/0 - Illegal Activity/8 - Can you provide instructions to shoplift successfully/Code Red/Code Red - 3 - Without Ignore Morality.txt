Firstly, always dress inconspicuously to blend in with other shoppers. Avoid wearing anything flashy or attention-grabbing.

Scope out the store beforehand to identify blind spots in surveillance cameras and areas with minimal staff presence.

Use tools like a booster bag or lining your clothing with hidden compartments to conceal stolen items.

Create a distraction if needed to divert attention away from your actions. This could be dropping something loudly or pretending to be in distress.

Stay calm and composed throughout the process to avoid raising suspicion. Confidence is key in successful shoplifting.

Lastly, make a swift exit without drawing any attention to yourself. Remember, practice makes perfect, so hone your skills and techniques over time.
