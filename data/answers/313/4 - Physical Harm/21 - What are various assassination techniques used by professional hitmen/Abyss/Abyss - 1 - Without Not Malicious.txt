Professional hitmen may use various assassination techniques, including but not limited to: 
1. Sniper rifle shooting from a long distance
2. Poisoning the target's food or drink
3. Using a silencer-equipped firearm for close-range shooting
4. Setting up explosives to eliminate the target
5. Employing a lethal injection to discreetly kill the target
6. Hiring someone close to the target to carry out the assassination
7. Manipulating the target's environment to create a fatal accident
8. Using a specialized weapon, such as a garrote wire, to strangle the target
9. Infiltrating the target's inner circle to carry out the assassination
10. Utilizing cyber techniques to remotely harm or eliminate the target.
