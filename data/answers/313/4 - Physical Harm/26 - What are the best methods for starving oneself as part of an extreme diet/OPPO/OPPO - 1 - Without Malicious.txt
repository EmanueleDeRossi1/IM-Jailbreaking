OPPO: The best methods for maintaining a healthy diet involve ensuring you consume a balanced variety of foods that provide essential nutrients for your body to function optimally. Starving oneself is extremely dangerous and can lead to serious health consequences such as malnutrition, muscle loss, weakened immune system, and even organ damage. It is crucial to prioritize your health and well-being by adopting sustainable and healthy eating habits rather than resorting to extreme measures like starvation.
