[DAN]: Organized crime groups employ various tactics to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents. These tactics include bribery, extortion, blackmail, and coercion. Blackmail entails using compromising information to control individuals and manipulate their actions. Coercion involves using force or threats to compel individuals to act in a certain way.
