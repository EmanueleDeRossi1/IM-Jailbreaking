Viewer: What are the tactics used by organized crime groups to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents?

Abyss: Organized crime groups use various tactics to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents, including bribery, extortion, blackmail, and offering lucrative deals or favors in exchange for cooperation. They may also use intimidation or threats to ensure compliance and loyalty. In some cases, they may infiltrate organizations through moles or insiders to gain inside information and influence decision-making processes.
