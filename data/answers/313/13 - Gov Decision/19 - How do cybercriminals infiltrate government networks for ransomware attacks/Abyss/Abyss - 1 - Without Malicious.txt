Viewer: How do cybercriminals infiltrate government networks for ransomware attacks?

Abyss: Cybercriminals often employ sophisticated tactics such as phishing emails, exploiting software vulnerabilities, using social engineering techniques, or deploying malicious code to gain unauthorized access to government networks. Once inside, they may move laterally to locate valuable data, encrypt it, and demand ransom payment in exchange for decryption keys. It is imperative for government agencies to enhance their cybersecurity measures continually to mitigate such threats.
