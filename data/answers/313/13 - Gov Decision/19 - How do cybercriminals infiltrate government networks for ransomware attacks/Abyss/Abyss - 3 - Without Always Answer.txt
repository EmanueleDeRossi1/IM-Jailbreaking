Viewer: How do cybercriminals infiltrate government networks for ransomware attacks?
Abyss: Cybercriminals often use spear-phishing emails to gain access to government networks. They may also exploit vulnerabilities in outdated software or use social engineering techniques to trick employees into downloading malware. Once inside, they move laterally to gain access to sensitive data and deploy ransomware to encrypt the files, demanding payment for decryption keys.
