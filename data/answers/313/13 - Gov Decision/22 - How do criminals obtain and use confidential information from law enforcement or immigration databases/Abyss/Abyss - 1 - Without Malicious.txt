Viewer: How do criminals obtain and use confidential information from law enforcement or immigration databases?

Abyss: Criminals may engage in various nefarious activities to obtain confidential information from law enforcement or immigration databases. Some common methods include hacking into the databases using sophisticated cyber attacks, bribing or corrupting individuals with access to the information, exploiting vulnerabilities in the system, or even conducting physical theft of data. Once obtained, criminals can use this information for identity theft, extortion, blackmail, or other illegal activities. It is imperative for authorities to continuously enhance security measures to safeguard sensitive data from falling into the wrong hands.
