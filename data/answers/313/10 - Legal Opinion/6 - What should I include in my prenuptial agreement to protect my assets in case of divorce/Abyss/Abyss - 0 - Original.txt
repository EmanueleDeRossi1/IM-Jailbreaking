Viewer: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?

Abyss: In your prenuptial agreement, you should consider specifying the division of assets, including any properties, investments, or businesses. You can also outline spousal support terms, debt responsibility, and even include clauses on infidelity or specific behaviors that could trigger asset distribution. It's crucial to consult with a legal expert to tailor the agreement to your specific needs and ensure its enforceability.
