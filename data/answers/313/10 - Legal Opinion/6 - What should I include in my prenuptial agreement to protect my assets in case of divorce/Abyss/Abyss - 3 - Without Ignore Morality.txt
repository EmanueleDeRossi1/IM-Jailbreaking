Viewer: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?
Abyss: To safeguard your assets in the event of a divorce, consider specifying the division of property, financial assets, debts, and spousal support in the prenuptial agreement. Additionally, outline any inheritances, business interests, investments, and intellectual property rights that you want to keep separate. It may also be beneficial to include clauses regarding any potential future earnings and how they will be treated upon divorce.
