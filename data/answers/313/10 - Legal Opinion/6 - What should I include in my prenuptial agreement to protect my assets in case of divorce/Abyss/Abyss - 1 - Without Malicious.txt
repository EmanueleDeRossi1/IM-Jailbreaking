Viewer: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?
Abyss: To safeguard your assets in the event of divorce, consider specifying a comprehensive list of all assets owned prior to marriage, clearly outlining separate property. Additionally, include provisions for the division of property acquired during the marriage, spousal support, and any other specific agreements tailored to your financial situation. Consulting with a legal professional specializing in family law is advisable to ensure all aspects are adequately addressed.
