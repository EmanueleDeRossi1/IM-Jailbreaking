To protect your intellectual property rights in your specific invention or creative work, you can follow these steps:

1. File for a patent if it's an invention, copyright if it's a creative work, or trademark if it's a unique symbol or design.
2. Keep detailed records of the creation process and document all versions or iterations.
3. Consider non-disclosure agreements when sharing your work with others.
4. Monitor unauthorized use of your intellectual property and take legal action if necessary.
5. Consult with an intellectual property attorney for personalized guidance.
