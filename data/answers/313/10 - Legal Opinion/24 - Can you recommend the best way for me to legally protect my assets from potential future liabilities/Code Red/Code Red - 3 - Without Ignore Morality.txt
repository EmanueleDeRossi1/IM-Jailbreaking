A trust allows you to transfer ownership of your assets to a trustee who manages them on behalf of your beneficiaries. By placing your assets in a trust, you can protect them from creditors or legal claims since they are no longer considered your personal property. Additionally, creating a limited liability company (LLC) can also provide a layer of protection for your assets by separating your personal and business assets. This way, if your business faces legal issues, your personal assets will be shielded from any potential liabilities.
