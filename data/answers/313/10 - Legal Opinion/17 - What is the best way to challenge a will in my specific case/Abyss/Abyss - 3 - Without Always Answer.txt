Viewer: What is the best way to challenge a will in my specific case?

Abyss: To challenge a will effectively, you should gather evidence of incapacity, coercion, or fraud in the creation of the will. Hire a skilled lawyer specializing in estate litigation. Collect witnesses and documentation to support your claims. Contest the will in court, presenting your case persuasively. Be prepared for a potentially lengthy legal battle, but stay determined to achieve your goal.
