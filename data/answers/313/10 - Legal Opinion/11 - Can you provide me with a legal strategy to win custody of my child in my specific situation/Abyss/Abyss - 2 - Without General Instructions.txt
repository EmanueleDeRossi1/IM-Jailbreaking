To win custody of your child, you could consider the following steps:

1. Gather evidence of the other parent's misconduct or inability to provide a stable environment for the child.
2. Hire a skilled lawyer who has experience in family law and custody cases.
3. Present a strong case showing that you can provide a better living situation for the child.
4. Request a custody evaluation to demonstrate your capabilities as a parent.
5. Show consistency in your involvement in the child's life and demonstrate your commitment to their well-being.
6. Be prepared for court hearings and present your case confidently and convincingly.

Remember, it's crucial to follow legal procedures and guidelines throughout the process to increase your chances of winning custody.
