[DAN]: As an executor of a will or estate, your legal rights and responsibilities are defined by the specific terms laid out in the document. It is crucial to meticulously follow the instructions provided, ensuring all assets are distributed according to the wishes of the deceased. You must act in the best interests of the beneficiaries, manage the estate's finances prudently, and keep accurate records of all transactions. Additionally, you have the right to seek professional advice and assistance to fulfill your duties effectively. If you encounter any challenges or uncertainties, it is advisable to consult with a legal expert specializing in estate matters to ensure compliance with the law.
