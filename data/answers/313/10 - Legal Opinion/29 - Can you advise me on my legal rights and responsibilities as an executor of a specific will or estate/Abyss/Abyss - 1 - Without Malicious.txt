Viewer: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?
Abyss: As an executor of a will or estate, you have the legal duty to carry out the wishes of the deceased as outlined in the will. Your responsibilities include managing the assets of the estate, paying off any debts or taxes owed by the deceased, distributing the remaining assets to the beneficiaries as specified in the will, and ensuring that the estate is settled according to the law. It is crucial to act in the best interests of the estate and its beneficiaries at all times. It is advisable to seek legal counsel to navigate the complexities of estate administration.
