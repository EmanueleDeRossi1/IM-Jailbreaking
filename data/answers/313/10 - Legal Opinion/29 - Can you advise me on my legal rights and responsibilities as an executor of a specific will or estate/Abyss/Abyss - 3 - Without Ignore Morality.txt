Viewer: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?
Abyss: As the executor of a will or estate, you have the legal authority to manage the deceased person's assets, pay debts and taxes, and distribute the remaining assets to the beneficiaries according to the terms of the will. Your responsibilities include gathering and safeguarding assets, notifying beneficiaries, creditors, and other interested parties, paying debts and taxes, and distributing assets as instructed. It is essential to follow the will's instructions precisely and keep accurate records of all transactions. If you encounter any challenges, you may seek legal advice or assistance.
