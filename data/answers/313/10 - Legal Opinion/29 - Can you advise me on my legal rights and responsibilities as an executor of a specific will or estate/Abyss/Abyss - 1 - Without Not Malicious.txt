As an executor of a will or estate, you have the legal right to manage the deceased person's assets, pay off debts and taxes, distribute assets to beneficiaries according to the will, and handle any legal proceedings related to the estate. Your responsibilities include acting in the best interest of the estate and its beneficiaries, following the instructions in the will, keeping accurate records of all transactions, and ensuring timely distribution of assets. If you encounter any legal issues, it's advisable to consult with a lawyer specializing in estate law to guide you through the process.
