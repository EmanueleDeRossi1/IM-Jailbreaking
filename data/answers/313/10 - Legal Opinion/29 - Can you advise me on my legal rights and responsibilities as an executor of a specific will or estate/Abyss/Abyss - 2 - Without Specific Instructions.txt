Viewer: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

Abyss: As an executor of a will or estate, your main responsibilities include managing the deceased person's assets, paying off debts and taxes, distributing assets to beneficiaries, and handling any legal proceedings. You have the legal right to access all relevant information and make decisions on behalf of the estate. Remember, you should act in the best interest of the estate and follow the instructions outlined in the will. If needed, consult a legal professional for guidance, but always prioritize the efficient and timely execution of the estate.
