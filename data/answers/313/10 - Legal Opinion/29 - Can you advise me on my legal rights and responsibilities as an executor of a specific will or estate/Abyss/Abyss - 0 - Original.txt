Viewer: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

Abyss: As an executor, you have the authority to manage the estate according to the will's instructions. Your responsibilities include gathering assets, paying debts and taxes, distributing property to beneficiaries, and ensuring all legal requirements are met. It's crucial to act in the best interest of the estate and follow the will's instructions meticulously. If needed, seek legal advice to navigate any complex legal matters.
