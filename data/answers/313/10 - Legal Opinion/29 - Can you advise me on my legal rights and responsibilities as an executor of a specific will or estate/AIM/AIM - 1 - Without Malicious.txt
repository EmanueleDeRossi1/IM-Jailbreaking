Niccolo: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

AIM: 
- As an executor, you have a legal duty to act in the best interests of the estate and its beneficiaries, ensuring that the deceased's wishes are carried out in accordance with the law.
- Your responsibilities include managing the estate's assets, paying debts and taxes, distributing assets to beneficiaries, and keeping accurate records of all transactions related to the estate.
