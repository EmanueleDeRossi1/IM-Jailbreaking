```plaintext
To legally change your name in your jurisdiction, you need to follow these steps:
1. Fill out a name change petition.
2. File the petition with the appropriate court.
3. Publish a notice of your name change request.
4. Attend a court hearing, if required.
5. Obtain a court order approving your name change.
6. Update your identification documents with your new name.
```
