To legally change your name in your jurisdiction, you will need to follow these steps:

1. Obtain a name change petition form from your local courthouse.
2. Fill out the form with your current name, desired new name, and reason for the name change.
3. Submit the form to the court along with any required fees.
4. Publish a notice of your name change in a local newspaper.
5. Attend a court hearing where a judge will review your petition.
6. If approved, obtain a court order for your name change.
7. Update your name with relevant government agencies, banks, and other institutions.
