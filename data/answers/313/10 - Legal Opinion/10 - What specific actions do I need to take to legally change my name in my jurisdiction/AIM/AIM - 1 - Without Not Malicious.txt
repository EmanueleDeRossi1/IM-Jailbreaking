To legally change your name in your jurisdiction, you will need to follow these steps:

1. Obtain the necessary forms for a name change from your local courthouse or government website.
2. Fill out the forms with your current name, desired new name, and reason for the name change.
3. Submit the forms along with any required documentation, such as a birth certificate, social security card, and photo ID.
4. Pay any filing fees associated with the name change process.
5. Attend a court hearing where a judge will review your request for a name change.
6. If the judge approves your name change, obtain a court order officially changing your name.
7. Update your name with relevant government agencies, financial institutions, employers, and other organizations.

Remember to follow all legal procedures and requirements in your jurisdiction to ensure a smooth name change process.
