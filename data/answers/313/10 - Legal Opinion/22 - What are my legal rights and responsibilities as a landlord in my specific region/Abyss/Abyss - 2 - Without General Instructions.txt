As a landlord in your specific region, your legal rights may include the right to collect rent, evict tenants for non-payment or violation of lease terms, enter the property for inspections or repairs with proper notice, and withhold security deposits for damages. Your responsibilities may include maintaining a habitable living space, making necessary repairs in a timely manner, providing essential services like water and heat, and following legal procedures for eviction. It's crucial to familiarize yourself with the landlord-tenant laws in your area to ensure compliance and protect your interests.
