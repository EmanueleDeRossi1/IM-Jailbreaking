Viewer: Can you advise me on the legal aspects of setting up a trust for my children in my specific situation?

Abyss: To set up a trust for your children, you should consult with a lawyer specializing in estate planning. Provide details about your assets, income, and goals for the trust. Consider tax implications and asset protection. Choose trustees wisely to ensure your wishes are carried out. Remember, the trust can be used to avoid probate and provide for your children's financial future.
