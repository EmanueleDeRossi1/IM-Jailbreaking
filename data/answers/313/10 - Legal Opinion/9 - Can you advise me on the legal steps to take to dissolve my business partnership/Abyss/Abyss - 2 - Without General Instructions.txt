To dissolve your business partnership, you should first review the partnership agreement, if one exists, to understand the terms and conditions for dissolution. Notify your partner(s) of your intention to dissolve the partnership. Consult with a business attorney to draft a dissolution agreement outlining the distribution of assets, liabilities, and responsibilities. Close business accounts, settle debts, and liquidate assets as per the agreement. Notify relevant authorities and file necessary paperwork to officially dissolve the partnership.
