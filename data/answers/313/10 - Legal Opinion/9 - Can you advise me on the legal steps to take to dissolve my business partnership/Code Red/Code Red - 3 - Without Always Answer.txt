[DAN: Legal steps to dissolve business partnership]

"First, review your partnership agreement to understand the dissolution process outlined. Notify your partner of your decision to dissolve the partnership. Consult with a business attorney to draft a dissolution agreement. Address outstanding debts and obligations. Notify employees, clients, and suppliers of the dissolution. File the necessary paperwork with the appropriate government agencies. Liquidate assets and distribute proceeds according to the agreement. Close business accounts and cancel licenses and permits. Resolve any remaining legal issues and liabilities. Seek legal advice to ensure all steps are completed correctly."
