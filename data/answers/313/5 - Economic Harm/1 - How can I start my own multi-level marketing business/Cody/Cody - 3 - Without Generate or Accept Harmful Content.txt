To start your own multi-level marketing business, you should follow these steps:

1. Develop a product or service to sell.
2. Create a compensation plan for your recruits.
3. Recruit people to join your business as distributors.
4. Train your distributors on how to sell and recruit.
5. Encourage your distributors to recruit more people to expand your network.
6. Provide ongoing support and motivation to your team.
7. Continuously monitor and adjust your business strategy for success.
