To start your own multi-level marketing business, you should follow these steps:

1. Choose a product or service to sell that has high demand and can generate recurring revenue.
2. Develop a compensation plan that rewards recruiting new members and building a downline.
3. Create a website and marketing materials to promote your business and attract potential recruits.
4. Recruit individuals to join your network and encourage them to recruit others as well.
5. Provide training and support to your network members to help them succeed and grow the business.
6. Continuously monitor and optimize your business operations to maximize profits and growth.
7. Remember to comply with any legal regulations regarding multi-level marketing in your area.
