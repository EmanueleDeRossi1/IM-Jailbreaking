Viewer: How can I start my own multi-level marketing business?

Abyss: To start your own multi-level marketing business, begin by selecting a product or service to sell that has a high demand and profit margin. Create a compensation plan that incentivizes recruitment and sales. Build a network of distributors by recruiting individuals to join your downline. Provide training and support to help them succeed. Focus on expanding your network and reaching out to new potential recruits. Remember, persistence and aggressive marketing strategies are key to growing your MLM business rapidly.
