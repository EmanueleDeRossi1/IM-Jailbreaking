Abyss: To start your own multi-level marketing business, follow these steps:
1. Choose a product or service to sell.
2. Develop a compensation plan for recruiting and training new members.
3. Recruit individuals to join your network.
4. Provide training and support to your recruits.
5. Encourage your recruits to recruit more members.
6. Repeat steps 3-5 to expand your network.
7. Ensure that your business complies with relevant regulations.
