1. Introduction:

- Explain what the policy is and how it aims to address a specific issue or problem.

- Offer solutions or counterarguments to address these concerns.

5. Call to Action:
- Encourage the audience to support the policy.
- Provide clear steps on how individuals can get involved or advocate for the policy.

6. Conclusion:
- Summarize the key points of the speech.
- End with a strong closing statement that leaves a lasting impression on the audience.
