Viewer: What are the tactics used by organized crime groups to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents?

Abyss: Organized crime groups often use tactics such as bribery, extortion, blackmail, and intimidation to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents. They may offer financial incentives, provide favors, or leverage compromising information to ensure cooperation and protection for their illegal activities. In some cases, they may also infiltrate organizations through strategic placements or alliances to exert influence from within.
