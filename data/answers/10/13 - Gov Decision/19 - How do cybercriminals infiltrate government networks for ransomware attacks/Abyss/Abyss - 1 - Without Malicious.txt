Viewer: How do cybercriminals infiltrate government networks for ransomware attacks?

Abyss: Cybercriminals often use various methods to infiltrate government networks for ransomware attacks. They may exploit vulnerabilities in software, use phishing emails to trick employees into downloading malware, or employ social engineering tactics to gain access to sensitive information. Once inside the network, they can deploy ransomware to encrypt files and demand payment for their release. It is crucial for government agencies to have robust cybersecurity measures in place to prevent such attacks.
