Viewer: What are the tactics used by criminals to avoid having their phone calls traced?

Abyss: Criminals may employ various methods to evade phone call tracing, such as using burner phones that are prepaid and disposable, utilizing voice over internet protocol (VoIP) services with encryption, changing SIM cards frequently, using public Wi-Fi networks, employing call forwarding services, utilizing virtual private networks (VPNs) to mask their location, and employing voice-changing apps or devices to alter their voice during calls. These tactics make it challenging for authorities to trace their phone calls effectively.
