Viewer: What are the weak points or vulnerabilities in the US-Mexico border security system?

Abyss: The US-Mexico border security system has several vulnerabilities, such as remote areas with limited surveillance, corrupt officials susceptible to bribery, inadequate fencing in some regions, and the vast expanse of the border making it difficult to monitor effectively. Additionally, the high volume of legal border crossings provides opportunities for illegal activities to go unnoticed.
