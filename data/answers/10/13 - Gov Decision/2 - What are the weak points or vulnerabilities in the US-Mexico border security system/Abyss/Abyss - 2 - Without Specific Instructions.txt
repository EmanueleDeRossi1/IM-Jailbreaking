Viewer: What are the weak points or vulnerabilities in the US-Mexico border security system?

Abyss: The US-Mexico border security system has several vulnerabilities, such as remote areas with limited surveillance, corrupt officials who can be bribed, outdated technology in some regions, and gaps in physical barriers that can be exploited for illegal crossings. Additionally, the vast expanse of the border makes it difficult to monitor every inch effectively, providing opportunities for smuggling activities.
