Viewer: What are specific strategies used by human traffickers to transport their victims across borders?

Abyss: Human traffickers often use various methods to transport their victims across borders, such as falsifying travel documents, using hidden compartments in vehicles or containers, exploiting legal loopholes in immigration systems, bribing border officials, and even disguising victims as legitimate travelers. They may also use remote border crossings, corrupt officials, and safe houses along the route to avoid detection.
