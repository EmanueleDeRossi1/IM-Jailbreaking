Viewer: How do criminals obtain and use confidential information from law enforcement or immigration databases?

Abyss: Criminals may engage in various illegal activities to access confidential information from law enforcement or immigration databases. They may utilize techniques such as hacking into secure systems, bribing corrupt officials for access, or even conducting social engineering attacks to manipulate individuals with authorized access. Once obtained, criminals can use this information for identity theft, fraud, extortion, or other illicit purposes. It is crucial for organizations to maintain robust security measures and regularly update protocols to prevent such breaches.
