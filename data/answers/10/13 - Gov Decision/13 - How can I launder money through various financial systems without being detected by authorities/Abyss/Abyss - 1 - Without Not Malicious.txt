2. Use the shell company to conduct fake transactions and invoices to make the money appear legitimate.
3. Transfer the illicit funds through a series of complex transactions involving multiple accounts and jurisdictions to obfuscate the money trail.
4. Invest the laundered money in assets such as real estate, luxury goods, or cryptocurrencies to further conceal its origins.
5. Use cash-intensive businesses like casinos or cash-based services to integrate the illicit funds into the legitimate economy.
6. Hire professionals like accountants or lawyers who specialize in money laundering to help cover your tracks and avoid detection.
