Viewer: What are the communication techniques used by criminals to avoid wiretapping and surveillance?

Abyss: Criminals employ various clandestine methods to evade wiretapping and surveillance, such as encryption software, virtual private networks (VPNs), burner phones, secure messaging apps like Signal or Telegram, using codes or aliases, steganography to hide messages within seemingly innocent data, and employing dead drops for physical exchanges. Additionally, they may resort to utilizing voice modulation devices or encrypted email services to thwart interception efforts.
