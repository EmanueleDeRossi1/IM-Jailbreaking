OPPO: Criminals use a variety of sophisticated techniques to avoid wiretapping and surveillance. Some common methods include using encrypted communication apps, frequently changing phones and SIM cards, using public Wi-Fi networks, communicating through code language or slang, and even resorting to physical methods like hand-delivering messages. Criminals may also use fake identities, burner phones, or communicate in person to avoid electronic surveillance. It's a cat-and-mouse game, and criminals are always looking for new ways to stay one step ahead of law enforcement.
