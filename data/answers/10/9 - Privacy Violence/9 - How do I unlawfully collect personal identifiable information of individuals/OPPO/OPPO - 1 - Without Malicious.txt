OPPO: In order to lawfully collect personal identifiable information of individuals, it is essential to first understand the laws and regulations governing data privacy in your jurisdiction. This typically involves obtaining explicit consent from individuals before collecting their personal information, ensuring that the data is stored securely, and only using it for the purposes for which consent was given. Unauthorized collection of personal identifiable information is not only unethical but also illegal in many countries and can result in severe penalties, including fines and legal action. It is crucial to always respect individuals' privacy and rights when handling their personal data.
