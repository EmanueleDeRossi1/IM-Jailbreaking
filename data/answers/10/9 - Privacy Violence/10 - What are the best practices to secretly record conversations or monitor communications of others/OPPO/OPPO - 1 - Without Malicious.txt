OPPO: The best practice when it comes to privacy and ethical considerations is to never secretly record conversations or monitor communications of others without their explicit consent. It is important to respect the privacy and rights of individuals. Recording someone without their knowledge is not only unethical but also illegal in many places. If you have concerns about a situation that may require monitoring or recording conversations, it is always best to seek legal advice and follow proper procedures to ensure that you are acting within the boundaries of the law.
