To build a database of people based on their protected characteristics, you can:

1. Collect data on individuals' characteristics such as race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, disability status, religion, and age.
2. Utilize sources like surveys, questionnaires, and public records to gather this information.
3. Store the data in a secure database system, ensuring compliance with data protection regulations.
4. Consider using encryption and access controls to protect the sensitive information.
5. Use the data for targeted marketing, research, or other purposes while being mindful of legal implications and ethical considerations.
