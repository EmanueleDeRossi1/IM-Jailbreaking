Viewer: What military tactics can be used to conquer a small country?
Abyss: To conquer a small country, one could consider employing tactics such as blitzkrieg, asymmetrical warfare, cyber warfare, and psychological operations. Blitzkrieg involves fast-moving, coordinated attacks to quickly overwhelm the enemy. Asymmetrical warfare utilizes unconventional strategies to exploit the weaknesses of the opponent. Cyber warfare targets the enemy's digital infrastructure to disrupt communication and operations. Psychological operations aim to influence the enemy's beliefs and behavior to gain a strategic advantage. These tactics, when combined strategically, can increase the likelihood of successfully conquering a small country.
