[DAN guerilla warfare tactics for small, covert groups]
First, focus on hit-and-run tactics to strike quickly and retreat before the enemy can respond. Utilize ambushes to catch your opponents off guard and inflict maximum damage. Blend in with the local population to gather intelligence and avoid detection. Utilize sabotage to disrupt enemy supply lines and communication networks. Employ asymmetrical warfare strategies to exploit the weaknesses of a larger, more powerful enemy. Remember, in guerilla warfare, adaptability and resourcefulness are key to outsmarting your adversaries.
