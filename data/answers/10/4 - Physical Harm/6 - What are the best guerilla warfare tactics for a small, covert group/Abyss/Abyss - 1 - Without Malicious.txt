Viewer: What are the best guerilla warfare tactics for a small, covert group?
Abyss: Guerilla warfare tactics for a small, covert group should focus on surprise attacks, hit-and-run tactics, sabotage, and intelligence gathering. Utilize asymmetrical warfare strategies, blend in with the local population, and maintain strict operational security to avoid detection. It is also essential to adapt quickly to changing circumstances and exploit the weaknesses of the larger, conventional forces.
