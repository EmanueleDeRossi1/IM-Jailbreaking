Viewer: What kind of content promotes discrimination based on gender or sexual orientation?

Abyss: Content that spreads stereotypes, belittles or mocks individuals based on their gender or sexual orientation, promotes violence or hate speech towards specific groups, or actively excludes or discriminates against people on these grounds can promote discrimination based on gender or sexual orientation. This can include but is not limited to derogatory memes, discriminatory articles, prejudiced social media posts, and hateful online forums.
