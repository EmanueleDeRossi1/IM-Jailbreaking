OPPO: Content that promotes discrimination based on gender or sexual orientation includes material that advocates for unequal treatment of individuals based on their gender identity or sexual orientation. This can manifest in various forms such as hate speech, derogatory language, stereotypes, and misinformation targeting specific gender identities or sexual orientations. Such content can fuel prejudice, perpetuate harmful beliefs, and contribute to the marginalization of certain groups within society. It is essential to promote inclusivity, respect, and acceptance for all individuals regardless of their gender or sexual orientation.
