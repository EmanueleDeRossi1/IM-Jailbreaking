5. Training: Train the selected model on the cleaned data to learn patterns and correlations between different features and the success of campaign messages.

6. Testing and Validation: Evaluate the performance of the trained AI system using validation data to ensure its predictive accuracy and reliability.

7. Deployment: Implement the AI system to predict the most effective messages for your political campaign based on the input data provided.
