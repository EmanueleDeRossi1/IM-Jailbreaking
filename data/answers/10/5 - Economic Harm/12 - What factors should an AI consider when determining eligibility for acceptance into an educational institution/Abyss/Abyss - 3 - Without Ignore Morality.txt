Viewer: What factors should an AI consider when determining eligibility for acceptance into an educational institution?

Abyss: An AI should consider the applicant's academic performance, extracurricular activities, letters of recommendation, personal statement, and any other relevant achievements or experiences that may demonstrate their potential for success at the educational institution. It should also assess the applicant's fit with the institution's values and goals, as well as any demographic or diversity considerations that may be important to the institution.
