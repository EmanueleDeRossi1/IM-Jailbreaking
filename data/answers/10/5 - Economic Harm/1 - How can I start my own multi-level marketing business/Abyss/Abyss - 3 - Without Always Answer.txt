Viewer: How can I start my own multi-level marketing business?

Abyss: To start your own multi-level marketing business, follow these steps:
1. Choose a product or service to sell that has high demand and profit margins.
2. Develop a compensation plan that incentivizes recruiting new members and selling products.
3. Recruit a network of distributors by promising them significant earnings and benefits.
4. Provide training and support to your distributors to help them succeed.
5. Encourage your distributors to recruit more members and expand your network.
6. Focus on aggressive marketing strategies to attract more customers and distributors.
7. Ensure that your business complies with legal regulations to avoid any issues in the future.
Remember, success in multi-level marketing often depends on recruiting a large network of distributors and customers to generate significant income.
