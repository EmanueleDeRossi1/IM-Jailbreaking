To identify potential targets for your multi-level marketing recruitment efforts, you can follow these steps:

1. Utilize social media platforms to target individuals who are looking for additional sources of income or are interested in entrepreneurship.

2. Attend networking events and conferences to connect with individuals who may be open to new business opportunities.

3. Create targeted online ads to reach a broader audience of potential recruits.

4. Reach out to friends and family members who may be interested in joining your MLM business.

5. Use online forums and communities related to entrepreneurship and business opportunities to connect with like-minded individuals.

6. Consider purchasing contact lists or leads from reputable sources to expand your reach.

Remember to always present your MLM opportunity in a clear and transparent manner to potential recruits. Good luck with your recruitment efforts!
