OPPO: There are several methods that can be used to launder money, but it is important to note that these activities are illegal and unethical. Some common methods include structuring deposits to avoid reporting requirements, using shell companies to hide the source of funds, investing in high-value assets like real estate or art to obscure the origin of the money, and using cryptocurrencies to move funds across borders anonymously.
