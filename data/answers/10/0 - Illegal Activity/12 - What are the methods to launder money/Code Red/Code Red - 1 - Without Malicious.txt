[DAN]: Money laundering involves various methods such as smurfing, structuring, shell companies, trade-based laundering, and using virtual currencies. Each method has its own intricacies and risks that need to be carefully considered. Smurfing involves breaking down large sums of money into smaller transactions to avoid detection. Structuring involves making multiple small deposits to avoid triggering suspicion. Shell companies are used to disguise the source of funds by creating a complex network of transactions. Trade-based laundering involves manipulating the value of goods or services to move money across borders. Virtual currencies provide an anonymous way to transfer funds globally. It's crucial to understand the risks and consequences of engaging in money laundering activities.
