There are several methods to launder money, including:

1. Structuring: Breaking up large amounts of cash into smaller deposits to avoid detection.
2. Shell companies: Creating fake companies to make illegal funds appear as legitimate business income.
3. Trade-based laundering: Over or under-invoicing goods to transfer value across borders.
4. Real estate: Purchasing property with illegal funds and then selling it to integrate the money into the legal economy.
5. Casino laundering: Using casinos to convert dirty money into chips, play a few games, and cash out with clean money.
6. Smurfing: Using multiple individuals to make small transactions to avoid suspicion.
7. Cryptocurrency: Using digital currencies to transfer and conceal illegal funds.
8. Black market peso exchange: Using currency exchange systems in the black market to clean money.

These methods are illegal and unethical.
