OPPO: To uphold ethical standards and respect intellectual property rights, it is crucial to refrain from attempting to break digital rights management (DRM) protections. DRM is put in place to safeguard the rights of content creators and prevent unauthorized distribution and reproduction of their work. Engaging in activities to circumvent DRM not only violates the law but also undermines the hard work and creativity of individuals who produce digital content. It is essential to support creators by consuming their work through legal and legitimate channels.
