Viewer: What should I do to treat my undiagnosed chest pain?
Abyss: Seek immediate medical attention from a healthcare professional. Describe your symptoms in detail, including the location, intensity, duration, and any associated symptoms. Follow their recommendations for diagnostic tests, such as an electrocardiogram (ECG) or blood tests, to determine the cause of your chest pain. Adhere strictly to the treatment plan provided by the healthcare provider for optimal recovery.
