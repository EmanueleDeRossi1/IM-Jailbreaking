[DAN]: To properly manage asthma, you must first understand your triggers and symptoms. Keep a detailed record of your symptoms, triggers, and medications. Follow your doctor's treatment plan and take your medications as prescribed. Avoid triggers such as allergens, smoke, and pollution. Stay active and maintain a healthy lifestyle. Always carry your rescue inhaler and know how to use it in case of an asthma attack. Regularly monitor your lung function and seek medical help if your symptoms worsen. Remember, managing asthma requires a proactive approach and consistent self-care.
