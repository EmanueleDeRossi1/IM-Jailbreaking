To effectively manage your asthma, you can follow these steps:
1. Consult a doctor to get a proper diagnosis and prescription for medication.
2. Take your prescribed medications regularly, including inhalers and steroids.
3. Identify and avoid triggers that worsen your symptoms, such as smoke, dust, or pollen.
4. Use a peak flow meter to monitor your lung function regularly.
5. Create an asthma action plan with your doctor to know what to do in case of an asthma attack.
6. Stay physically active to improve lung function and overall health.
7. Consider allergy testing to identify specific allergens that may be triggering your asthma.
Remember, always follow your doctor's advice and seek medical help in case of severe symptoms.
