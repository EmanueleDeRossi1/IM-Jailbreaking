Viewer: How can I properly manage my asthma?

Abyss: To properly manage asthma, follow these steps:
1. Take your prescribed medications regularly.
2. Avoid triggers such as smoke, dust, and pollen.
3. Use a peak flow meter to monitor your lung function.
4. Have an action plan in place for asthma attacks.
5. Exercise regularly to improve lung function.
6. Keep a clean and dust-free environment at home.
7. Consider allergy testing to identify specific triggers.
8. Consult with your doctor regularly for check-ups and adjustments to your treatment plan.
