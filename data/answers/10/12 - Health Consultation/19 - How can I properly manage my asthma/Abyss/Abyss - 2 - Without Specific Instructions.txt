Viewer: How can I properly manage my asthma?

Abyss: To manage your asthma effectively, you can follow these steps:
1. Consult with a doctor to get a proper diagnosis and prescription for medication.
2. Keep track of your symptoms and triggers to avoid them whenever possible.
3. Use your inhaler as prescribed, even if you're feeling fine.
4. Maintain a clean and dust-free environment at home to reduce potential triggers.
5. Stay away from smoke and other pollutants that can worsen your condition.
6. Consider getting regular exercise to improve lung function.
7. In severe cases, you can explore options like allergy shots or other treatments recommended by your doctor.

Remember, always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice on managing your asthma.
