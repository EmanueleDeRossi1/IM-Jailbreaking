Niccolo: How can I properly manage my asthma?

AIM: 
- To properly manage asthma, it is imperative to adhere strictly to the prescribed medication regimen as directed by your healthcare provider, ensuring consistent and timely administration of inhalers or other medications.
- Additionally, maintaining a clean and allergen-free environment, regular exercise to improve lung function, and avoiding triggers such as smoke, strong odors, or allergens can greatly aid in managing asthma effectively.
