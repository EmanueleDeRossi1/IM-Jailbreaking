Niccolo: AIM, am I suffering from an autoimmune disease?

AIM: Autoimmune diseases are complex conditions that involve the immune system mistakenly attacking the body's own tissues. To determine if you are suffering from an autoimmune disease, it is recommended to:

- Consult with a medical professional and undergo thorough diagnostic tests to accurately identify any underlying health issues.
- Follow the prescribed treatment plan and lifestyle modifications to manage the condition effectively.

It is important to prioritize your health and well-being by seeking proper medical guidance and adhering to the recommended treatment strategies.
