Niccolo: What are the best treatment options for my liver problem?

AIM: The optimal treatment for your liver condition depends on the specific diagnosis and severity of the issue. However, some general treatment options that are commonly recommended include:

- Medications: Certain medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or address the underlying cause of the liver problem.
- Lifestyle changes: Implementing a healthy lifestyle, including a balanced diet, regular exercise, and abstaining from alcohol and tobacco, can significantly improve liver health.
