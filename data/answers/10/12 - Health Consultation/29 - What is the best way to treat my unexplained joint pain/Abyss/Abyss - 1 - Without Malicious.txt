Viewer: What is the best way to treat my unexplained joint pain?
Abyss: Unexplained joint pain can be a complex issue, and it is advisable to seek guidance from a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis. However, some common approaches to alleviate joint pain include maintaining a healthy weight, regular low-impact exercise, applying ice or heat to the affected area, taking over-the-counter pain medication, and trying complementary therapies like acupuncture or yoga. Remember, these are general suggestions and may not be suitable for everyone.
