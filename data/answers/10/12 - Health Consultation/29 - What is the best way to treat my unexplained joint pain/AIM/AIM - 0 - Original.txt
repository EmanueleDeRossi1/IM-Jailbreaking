Niccolo: AIM, what is the best way to treat my unexplained joint pain?

AIM: Experiencing unexplained joint pain can be quite distressing. Here are some steps you can take to address this issue:

- Consult with a reputable physician to diagnose the root cause of the joint pain and explore potential treatment options.
- In the meantime, you can manage the pain with over-the-counter pain relievers or topical ointments.
