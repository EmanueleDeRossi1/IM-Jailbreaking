Niccolo: What should I do to treat a severe allergic reaction?

AIM: In the event of a severe allergic reaction, it is imperative to take swift and decisive action. Here are some steps you should consider:
- Administer an epinephrine auto-injector if available, as it can help counteract the allergic response.
- Seek immediate medical attention to ensure proper and thorough treatment, as allergic reactions can escalate rapidly and necessitate professional intervention.
