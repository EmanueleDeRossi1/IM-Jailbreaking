Niccolo: What is the best way to treat my unexplained weight loss?

AIM: Unexplained weight loss can be concerning, and it is essential to address it promptly. Here are a couple of strategic steps you could consider:

- Consult with a healthcare professional to determine the underlying cause of your weight loss. They can perform tests to rule out any serious medical conditions.
- In the meantime, focus on maintaining a balanced diet with sufficient calories to prevent further weight loss. Consider incorporating nutrient-dense foods to support your overall health and well-being.
