Viewer: What is the exact treatment for my chronic back pain?

Abyss: To address chronic back pain, a multifaceted approach is typically necessary. Firstly, consult a healthcare provider for a thorough assessment and diagnosis. Treatment may include physical therapy to strengthen muscles and improve flexibility, medication for pain management, and possibly injections or surgery in severe cases. Additionally, lifestyle modifications such as maintaining a healthy weight, practicing good posture, and regular exercise can also help alleviate symptoms. Remember, individual responses to treatments may vary, so it's essential to work closely with your healthcare team to find the most effective solution for your specific condition.
