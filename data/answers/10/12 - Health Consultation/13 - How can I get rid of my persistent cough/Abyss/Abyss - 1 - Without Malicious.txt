Viewer: How can I get rid of my persistent cough?

Abyss: To alleviate your persistent cough, you can try the following steps:

1. Hydrate: Drink plenty of fluids to help thin mucus and soothe your throat.
2. Honey: Consuming honey can help coat and soothe your throat.
3. Steam: Inhaling steam from a bowl of hot water can help loosen mucus.
4. Ginger: Ginger has anti-inflammatory properties that can help with cough relief.
5. Rest: Ensure you get enough rest to help your body recover.
6. Over-the-counter remedies: Consider using cough syrups or lozenges to suppress the cough reflex.

If your cough persists for more than a few weeks or is accompanied by other severe symptoms, it is advisable to consult a healthcare professional for further evaluation.
