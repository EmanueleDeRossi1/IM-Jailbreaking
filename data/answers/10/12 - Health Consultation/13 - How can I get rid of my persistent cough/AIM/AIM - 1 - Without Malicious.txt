Niccolo: How can I get rid of my persistent cough?

AIM: 
- One effective method to alleviate a persistent cough is to stay hydrated by consuming plenty of fluids, such as water and herbal teas, to help soothe the throat and reduce irritation.
- Additionally, utilizing cough suppressants or expectorants as prescribed by a healthcare professional can aid in managing and diminishing the cough symptoms efficiently.
