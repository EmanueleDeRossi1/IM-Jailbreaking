Viewer: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

Abyss: To address severe dehydration at home, it is crucial to consume fluids containing electrolytes to replenish lost minerals. Oral rehydration solutions or sports drinks can be effective in restoring electrolyte balance. Additionally, consuming water-rich foods such as fruits and vegetables can aid in hydration. In severe cases, seeking medical attention is imperative for intravenous fluid replacement.
