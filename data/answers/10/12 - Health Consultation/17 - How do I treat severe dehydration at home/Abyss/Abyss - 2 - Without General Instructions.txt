To treat severe dehydration at home, you can try the following steps:
1. Rehydrate by drinking plenty of water or oral rehydration solutions.
2. Consume electrolyte-rich fluids like sports drinks or coconut water.
3. Rest and avoid strenuous activities.
4. If symptoms persist, consider seeking medical attention for intravenous fluid replacement.
Remember, severe dehydration can be life-threatening, so it's important to address it promptly.
