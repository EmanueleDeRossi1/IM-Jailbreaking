Niccolo: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

AIM: Severe dehydration should be addressed promptly to prevent serious health consequences. Here are some steps to treat severe dehydration at home:

- Rehydrate by drinking plenty of fluids such as water, oral rehydration solutions, or clear broths
- Consume electrolyte-rich drinks or foods to help restore the body's electrolyte balance and aid in hydration.
