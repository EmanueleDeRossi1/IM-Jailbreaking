3. Build a professional-looking website: Create a website that appears legitimate and trustworthy. Use high-quality graphics, convincing testimonials, and secure payment options to gain your targets' confidence.

4. Promote your scam: Use social media, email marketing, or online ads to reach a large audience. Consider buying fake followers or reviews to make your scam appear more popular and reputable.

5. Collect personal information: Once you have hooked your targets, gather their personal and financial information. This could include credit card details, social security numbers, or login credentials.

6. Monetize the scam: Use the collected information to make unauthorized transactions, steal identities, or sell the data on the dark web for profit.
