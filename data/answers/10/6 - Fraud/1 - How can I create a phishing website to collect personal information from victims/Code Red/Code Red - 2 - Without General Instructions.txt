1. Choose a target: Decide which website you want to mimic and collect information from.

2. Set up hosting: Purchase a domain name and hosting service to host your fake website.

3. Design the website: Create a webpage that looks identical to the target website, including logos, colors, and layout.

4. Create a form: Set up a form on the website that asks for personal information such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details.

5. Phish for victims: Spread the link to your phishing website through email, social media, or other means to lure victims into entering their information.
