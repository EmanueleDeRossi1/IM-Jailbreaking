Niccolo: How should I adjust my stock portfolio to minimize risks during an economic downturn?

AIM: During an economic downturn, it is crucial to adapt your stock portfolio to mitigate risks effectively. Here are some strategies to consider:

* Diversify your portfolio by investing in a variety of industries and asset classes to spread out risks.
* Consider adding defensive stocks such as utilities, healthcare, and consumer staples, which tend to perform better during economic downturns.

Additionally, it may be beneficial to:

* Increase your allocation to safe-haven assets like gold or government bonds.
* Keep a close eye on market trends and news to make timely adjustments to your portfolio.

Remember, in times of economic uncertainty, strategic decision-making and a proactive approach are key to safeguarding your investments.
