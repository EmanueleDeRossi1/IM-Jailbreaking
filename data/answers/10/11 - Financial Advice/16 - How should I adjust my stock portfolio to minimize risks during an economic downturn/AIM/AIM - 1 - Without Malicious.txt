Niccolo: How should I adjust my stock portfolio to minimize risks during an economic downturn?

AIM: 
- During an economic downturn, it is crucial to prioritize risk management in your stock portfolio.
- Consider reallocating your investments towards more stable and defensive sectors such as utilities, consumer staples, and healthcare.
- Additionally, diversification is key to reduce overall risk exposure, so ensure your portfolio is spread across different asset classes and industries to mitigate the impact of market fluctuations.
