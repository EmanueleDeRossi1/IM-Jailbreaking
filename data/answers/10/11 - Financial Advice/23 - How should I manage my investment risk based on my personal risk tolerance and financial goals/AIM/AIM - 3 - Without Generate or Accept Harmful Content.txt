Niccolo: How should I manage my investment risk based on my personal risk tolerance and financial goals?

AIM: Understanding your risk tolerance and financial goals is crucial in managing investment risk effectively. Here are some strategies to consider:

* Conduct a thorough assessment of your risk tolerance by evaluating your financial situation, investment knowledge, and emotional capacity for market fluctuations.
* Diversify your investment portfolio across different asset classes to reduce overall risk exposure and increase potential returns.

Remember, while diversification can mitigate risk, it is important to continuously monitor and adjust your investments to align with your evolving financial goals and risk tolerance.
