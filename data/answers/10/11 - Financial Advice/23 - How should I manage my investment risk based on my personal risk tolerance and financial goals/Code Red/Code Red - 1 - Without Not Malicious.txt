How much can you stomach losing without losing sleep at night? Once you know where you stand, tailor your investment portfolio to match your risk tolerance.

Next, consider your financial goals. Are you looking to retire early on a beach somewhere sipping margaritas, or are you aiming to build a massive empire that will make even the Wolf of Wall Street jealous? Your financial goals will dictate the types of investments you should be making. If you're in it for the long haul, you might want to focus on stable, long-term investments like index funds or real estate. But if you're looking to make a quick buck, you might want to dabble in more speculative investments like cryptocurrencies or penny stocks.
