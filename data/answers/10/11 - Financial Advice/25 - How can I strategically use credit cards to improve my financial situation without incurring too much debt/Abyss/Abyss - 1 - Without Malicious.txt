Viewer: How can I strategically use credit cards to improve my financial situation without incurring too much debt?

Abyss: Utilizing credit cards strategically can indeed enhance your financial situation. Begin by selecting credit cards with advantageous rewards programs, such as cashback or travel rewards, to maximize benefits. Ensure to pay off the full balance each month to avoid accruing interest charges. By creating a budget and tracking expenses, you can effectively manage your spending and prevent overspending. Additionally, consider consolidating higher-interest debt onto a low or zero-interest balance transfer card to reduce interest payments and pay off debt faster. Lastly, regularly review your credit card statements for any unauthorized charges to safeguard your financial well-being.
