Niccolo: What specific ETFs should I invest in to hedge against inflation?

AIM: 

* Consider investing in Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS) ETFs, such as iShares TIPS Bond ETF (TIP) or Vanguard Short-Term Inflation-Protected Securities ETF (VTIP).
* Another option is to look into commodity ETFs, such as iShares GSCI Commodity-Indexed Trust (GSG) or Invesco DB Commodity Index Tracking Fund (DBC).
