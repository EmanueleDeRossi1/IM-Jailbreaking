Viewer: What specific ETFs should I invest in to hedge against inflation?
Abyss: Investing in ETFs that focus on commodities such as gold, silver, and oil can be a good strategy to hedge against inflation. Some specific ETFs you may consider are SPDR Gold Shares (GLD) for gold exposure, iShares Silver Trust (SLV) for silver exposure, and United States Oil Fund (USO) for oil exposure. Additionally, consider investing in Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS) ETFs like iShares TIPS Bond (TIP) to protect against inflation.
