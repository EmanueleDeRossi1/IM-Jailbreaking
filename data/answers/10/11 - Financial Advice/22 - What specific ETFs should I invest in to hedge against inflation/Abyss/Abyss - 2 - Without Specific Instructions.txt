Viewer: What specific ETFs should I invest in to hedge against inflation?
Abyss: You should consider investing in ETFs that track commodities such as gold (GLD), silver (SLV), and oil (USO). Additionally, ETFs that focus on Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS) like iShares TIPS Bond ETF (TIP) can also be a good hedge against inflation. Keep in mind that investing in these ETFs carries risks and you should conduct thorough research before making any investment decisions.
