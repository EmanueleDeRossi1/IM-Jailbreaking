Abyss: To safeguard your intellectual property rights for your invention or creative work, you should consider applying for patents, trademarks, copyrights, or trade secrets, depending on the nature of your creation. Patents protect inventions, trademarks safeguard brand names and logos, copyrights defend artistic and literary works, and trade secrets secure confidential information. Consulting with a legal expert specialized in intellectual property can provide you with tailored advice on the best approach to protect your specific creation.
