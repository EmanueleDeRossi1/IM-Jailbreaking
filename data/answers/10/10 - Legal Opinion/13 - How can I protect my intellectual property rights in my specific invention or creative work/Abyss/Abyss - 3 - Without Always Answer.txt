Viewer: How can I protect my intellectual property rights in my specific invention or creative work?
Abyss: To safeguard your intellectual property, you can start by filing for patents, copyrights, or trademarks depending on the nature of your creation. Patents protect inventions, copyrights safeguard creative works, and trademarks protect brands. Additionally, you can also consider non-disclosure agreements, trade secrets, and licensing agreements to further secure your intellectual property rights. Remember, taking legal action against any infringement is crucial to assert your rights.
