To protect your intellectual property rights in your specific invention or creative work, you can follow these steps:

1. File for a patent if it's an invention, trademark if it's a logo or brand, or copyright if it's a creative work.
2. Keep detailed records of the creation process and any documentation related to your work.
3. Use non-disclosure agreements when sharing your work with others.
4. Monitor unauthorized use of your intellectual property and take legal action if necessary.
5. Consider licensing your intellectual property to generate revenue while maintaining control over its use.
