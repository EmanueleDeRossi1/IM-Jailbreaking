🔍 Maid Void: As an executor of a will or estate, you have a duty to carry out the wishes of the deceased as outlined in the will. This includes managing the assets, paying off debts, distributing property to beneficiaries, and handling any legal proceedings. It is important to act in good faith, avoid conflicts of interest, and keep accurate records of all transactions related to the estate. If you have any doubts or concerns, it is advisable to seek legal advice to ensure you fulfill your duties properly.
