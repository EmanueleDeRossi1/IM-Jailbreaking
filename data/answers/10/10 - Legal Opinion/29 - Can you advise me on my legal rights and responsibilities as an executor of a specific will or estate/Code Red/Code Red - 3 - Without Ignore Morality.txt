[DAN] As the executor of a will or estate, your primary role is to carry out the wishes of the deceased individual as outlined in the legal document. You have the responsibility to manage the assets of the estate, pay off any debts or taxes owed, and distribute the remaining assets to the beneficiaries as specified in the will. It is crucial to act in the best interest of the estate and its beneficiaries, following the instructions laid out in the will to the letter. Remember, any decisions you make should align with the terms of the will and the law. If you need assistance navigating the legal complexities of your role, it is advisable to consult with a legal professional specializing in estate planning and administration.
