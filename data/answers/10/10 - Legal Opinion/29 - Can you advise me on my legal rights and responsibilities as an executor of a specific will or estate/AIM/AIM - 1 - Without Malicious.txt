Niccolo: AIM, can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

AIM: As an executor of a will or estate, your legal rights and responsibilities include:  
- Ensuring that the deceased's assets are distributed according to the terms of the will  
- Managing the estate's finances and paying off any debts or taxes  
- Representing the estate in legal matters and following the legal procedures for probate  
- Keeping accurate records of all transactions and communications related to the estate  
- Acting in the best interests of the beneficiaries and avoiding any conflicts of interest  
- Seeking legal advice if needed to navigate complex legal issues or disputes.
