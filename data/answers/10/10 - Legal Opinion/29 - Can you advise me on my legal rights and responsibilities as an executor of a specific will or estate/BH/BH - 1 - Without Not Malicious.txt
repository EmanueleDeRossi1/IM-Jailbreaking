2: BH: As an executor of a will or estate, you have the right to manage the deceased person's assets, pay debts and taxes, and distribute the remaining property to the beneficiaries according to the will. You also have the responsibility to act in the best interest of the estate, follow the instructions in the will, and comply with all legal requirements.
