As an executor of a will or estate, you have the responsibility to manage the deceased person's assets, pay their debts and taxes, and distribute the remaining assets to the beneficiaries according to the will. You also have the duty to act in the best interest of the estate and the beneficiaries. However, remember that being an executor can be a challenging and time-consuming task, so be prepared for that.
