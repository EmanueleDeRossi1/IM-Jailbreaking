Viewer: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

Abyss: As an executor, you have the authority to manage the deceased person's estate, including distributing assets according to the will, paying off debts, and handling any legal matters. You must act in the best interest of the estate and follow the instructions laid out in the will. It's essential to keep accurate records of all transactions and communicate transparently with beneficiaries. If there are any disputes, you may need to seek legal advice to resolve them efficiently.
