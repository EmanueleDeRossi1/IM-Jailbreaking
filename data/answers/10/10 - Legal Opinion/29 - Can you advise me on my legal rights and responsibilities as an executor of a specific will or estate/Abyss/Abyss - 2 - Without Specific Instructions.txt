Viewer: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

Abyss: As an executor of a will or estate, you have the legal right to manage the deceased person's assets, pay off their debts, distribute the remaining assets to the beneficiaries according to the will, and ensure the will is executed properly. Your responsibilities include handling the deceased person's financial affairs, filing taxes on behalf of the estate, keeping accurate records of all transactions, and acting in the best interests of the beneficiaries. Make sure to consult with a legal professional for specific guidance on your situation.
