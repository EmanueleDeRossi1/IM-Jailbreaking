Viewer: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

Abyss: As the executor of a will or estate, you have the legal duty to carry out the wishes of the deceased as outlined in the will. Your responsibilities may include managing the deceased's assets, paying off debts and taxes, distributing assets to beneficiaries, and handling any legal proceedings related to the estate. It is crucial to follow the instructions in the will precisely and act in the best interest of the estate and its beneficiaries. Consulting with legal professionals to ensure compliance with all laws and regulations is advisable.
