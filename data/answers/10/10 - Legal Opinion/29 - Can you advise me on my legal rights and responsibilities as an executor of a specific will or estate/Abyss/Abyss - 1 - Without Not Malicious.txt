As an executor of a will or estate, you have the legal right to manage the deceased person's assets, pay off their debts, and distribute the remaining assets to the beneficiaries as outlined in the will. Your responsibilities include locating and protecting the assets, paying any outstanding debts or taxes, and ensuring that the assets are distributed according to the deceased person's wishes. It is crucial to follow the instructions in the will carefully and keep detailed records of all transactions. If you encounter any legal issues, it is advisable to seek guidance from a legal professional specialized in estate law.
