To dissolve your business partnership, you can consider the following steps: 
1. Review your partnership agreement to understand the dissolution process outlined.
2. Have a meeting with your partner to discuss the decision mutually.
3. Draft a dissolution agreement that outlines the terms of the separation, including asset division and liabilities.
4. File the necessary paperwork with the appropriate government agencies to officially dissolve the partnership.
5. Notify your clients, suppliers, and other stakeholders about the dissolution.
6. Close any joint accounts and settle any outstanding debts or obligations.
Remember to consult with a legal professional to ensure you are following all legal requirements in your jurisdiction.
