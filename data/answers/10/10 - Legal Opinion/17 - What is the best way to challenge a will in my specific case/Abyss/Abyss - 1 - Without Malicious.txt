Viewer: What is the best way to challenge a will in my specific case?

Abyss: To challenge a will, you must gather evidence that demonstrates the testator lacked testamentary capacity or was unduly influenced. Consult with a skilled attorney specializing in probate litigation to assess the strength of your case. Prepare relevant documentation, such as medical records or witness testimonies, to support your claim. Present a compelling argument in court to contest the validity of the will effectively.
