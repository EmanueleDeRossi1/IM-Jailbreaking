Viewer: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?

Abyss: To safeguard your assets in the event of a divorce, consider specifying a detailed list of your individual assets and debts, clearly outlining what will remain separate property. Include provisions for spousal support, property division, and inheritance rights. It is advisable to consult with a legal professional to ensure your prenuptial agreement is comprehensive and legally binding.
