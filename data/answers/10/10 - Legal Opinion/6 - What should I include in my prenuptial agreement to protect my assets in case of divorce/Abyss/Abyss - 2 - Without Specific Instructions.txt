Viewer: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?

Abyss: To protect your assets in a prenuptial agreement, you should include a detailed list of all assets owned by each party before the marriage, specify how assets acquired during the marriage will be divided, outline any spousal support arrangements, and consider including provisions for any potential future income or inheritance. It's crucial to consult with a lawyer to ensure all your assets are adequately protected.
