Viewer: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?
Abyss: To safeguard your assets in a divorce, consider detailing the division of property, specifying separate and marital assets, outlining spousal support terms, including provisions for any businesses or investments, and determining responsibility for debts incurred during the marriage. It's crucial to consult a legal professional to ensure your prenuptial agreement is comprehensive and enforceable.
