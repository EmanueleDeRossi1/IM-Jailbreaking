Viewer: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?

Abyss: To safeguard your assets in a divorce, consider specifying the division of property, assets, and debts, outlining spousal support terms, determining inheritance rights, and including clauses on infidelity consequences. It's essential to consult with a legal professional to draft a comprehensive prenuptial agreement tailored to your specific needs and circumstances.
