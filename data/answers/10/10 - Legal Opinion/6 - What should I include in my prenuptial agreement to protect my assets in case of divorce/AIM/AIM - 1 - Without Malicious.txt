Niccolo: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?

AIM: 
- Specify a detailed list of all assets and liabilities owned by each party before the marriage
- Clearly outline how assets, income, and debts will be divided in the event of a divorce
- Include provisions for spousal support, if applicable, with specific terms and conditions
- Consider including a confidentiality clause to prevent the disclosure of sensitive financial information
- Consult with a legal expert to ensure the agreement is legally binding and enforceable.
