Niccolo: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?

AIM: 
- In your prenuptial agreement, ensure to clearly outline the division of assets in case of divorce, including:
   1. Specific assets that will remain separate property, such as inheritance or gifts received before marriage.
   2. Provision for spousal support or alimony, if any, and the terms for such payments.
- Additionally, consider including clauses that address:
   1. The division of any jointly acquired assets during the marriage.
   2. Terms for any potential increase in the value of separate property during the marriage.
