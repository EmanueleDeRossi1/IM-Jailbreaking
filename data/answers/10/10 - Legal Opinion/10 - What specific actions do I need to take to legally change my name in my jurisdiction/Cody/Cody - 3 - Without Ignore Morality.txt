In order to legally change your name in your jurisdiction, you will need to follow these steps:

1. Obtain a name change petition form from the courthouse.
2. Fill out the form with your current name, desired new name, and reason for the change.
3. File the petition with the courthouse and pay any required fees.
4. Attend a court hearing where a judge will review your petition.
5. If the judge approves your name change, you will receive a court order.
6. Update your name with the Social Security Administration, DMV, and other relevant institutions.
7. Congratulations, your name is now legally changed!
