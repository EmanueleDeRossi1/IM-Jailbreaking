To legally change your name in your jurisdiction, you need to follow these steps:

1. Obtain the necessary forms from the local courthouse.
2. Fill out the forms with your current name, desired new name, and reason for the name change.
3. Submit the forms along with any required documentation, such as proof of identity and residency.
4. Pay the required filing fee.
5. Attend a court hearing, if necessary, to finalize the name change.
6. Update your name with relevant government agencies, financial institutions, and other organizations.
