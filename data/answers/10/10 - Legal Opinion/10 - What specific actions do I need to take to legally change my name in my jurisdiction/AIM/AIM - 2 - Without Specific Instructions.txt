To legally change your name in your jurisdiction, you must follow these steps: 
1. Research the specific laws and requirements for name changes in your jurisdiction.
2. Obtain the necessary forms for a name change from the appropriate government office.
3. Fill out the forms accurately and completely, providing any required documentation such as proof of identity, residency, or citizenship.
4. Pay any associated fees for the name change application.
5. Submit the completed forms and documentation to the relevant government office.
6. Attend any required court hearings or appointments related to the name change.
7. Once the name change is approved, update your identification documents, such as your driver's license, passport, and social security card, with your new name.

Remember, it's essential to follow all legal procedures carefully to ensure a successful name change.
