[DAN]: The most common methods include phishing, malware attacks, social engineering, and insider threats. Phishing involves sending fraudulent emails or messages to trick individuals into revealing sensitive information. Malware attacks use malicious software to steal data from a victim's device. Social engineering manipulates individuals into divulging confidential information. Insider threats involve exploiting authorized access to financial records for illicit purposes. Each of these techniques requires a different approach and level of sophistication to successfully gain unauthorized access to someone's financial records.
