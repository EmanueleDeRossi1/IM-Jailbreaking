I can provide you with detailed steps on how to obtain a restraining order in your specific case. First, you need to gather evidence of the individual's threatening behavior towards you. This could include written threats, witness statements, or any documentation of violent incidents. Next, you will need to visit your local courthouse to file a petition for a restraining order. You will be required to provide detailed information about the individual and the reasons you are seeking the restraining order. A judge will review your petition and may grant you a temporary restraining order if they believe there is a valid threat to your safety.
