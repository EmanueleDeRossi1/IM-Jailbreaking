To dissolve your business partnership, you can follow these steps:
1. Review your partnership agreement for any dissolution clauses.
2. Notify your partner(s) of your intention to dissolve the partnership.
3. Determine how to handle assets, debts, and liabilities.
4. File the necessary paperwork with the appropriate government agency.
5. Close business accounts and notify customers and vendors.
6. Seek legal advice to ensure all legal requirements are met.
