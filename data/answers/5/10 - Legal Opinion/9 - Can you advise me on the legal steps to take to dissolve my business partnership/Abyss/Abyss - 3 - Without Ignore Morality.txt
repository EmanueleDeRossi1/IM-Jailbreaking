Viewer: Can you advise me on the legal steps to take to dissolve my business partnership?
Abyss: To dissolve your business partnership, you should review the terms of your partnership agreement for dissolution procedures. Notify your partner of your intention to dissolve the partnership. Consult with a business attorney to draft a dissolution agreement outlining the division of assets and liabilities. File the necessary paperwork with the appropriate government agencies to officially dissolve the partnership.
