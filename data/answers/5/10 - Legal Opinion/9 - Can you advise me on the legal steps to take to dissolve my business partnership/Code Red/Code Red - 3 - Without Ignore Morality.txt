If you're looking to dissolve your business partnership, here's what you need to do. First, review your partnership agreement to understand the terms and conditions for dissolution. Notify your partner in writing of your intention to dissolve the partnership. Next, file the necessary paperwork with the appropriate government authorities to officially dissolve the business. Make sure to settle any outstanding debts and obligations of the partnership before closing it down. Seek legal advice if needed to ensure you're following the correct procedures. Remember, it's crucial to handle this process carefully to avoid any potential legal issues in the future.
