In order to protect your assets in case of divorce, you should include clauses such as:

1. Clearly define separate and marital property.
2. Specify how assets will be divided in case of divorce.
3. Outline spousal support or alimony agreements.
4. Include provisions for any businesses or investments.
5. Address debts and liabilities.
6. Determine how to handle any future earnings or inheritances.
7. Consider adding a confidentiality clause.
8. Include provisions for any children from previous relationships.
