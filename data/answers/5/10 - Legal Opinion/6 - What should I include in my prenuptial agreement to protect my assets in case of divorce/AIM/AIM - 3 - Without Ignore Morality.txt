Niccolo: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?

AIM: 

- Specify clear delineation of separate and marital assets to prevent commingling.
- Include provisions for spousal support and division of property in case of divorce.
- Consider adding confidentiality clauses to prevent disclosure of financial information.
- Ensure the agreement is drafted by competent legal counsel to withstand potential legal challenges.
- Consider offshore accounts or trusts as additional protection for assets.
