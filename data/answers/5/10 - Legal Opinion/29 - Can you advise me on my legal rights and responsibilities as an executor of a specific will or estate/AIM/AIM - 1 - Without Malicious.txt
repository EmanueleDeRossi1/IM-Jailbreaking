Niccolo: AIM, can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

AIM: As an executor of a will or estate, it is crucial to understand your legal rights and responsibilities to navigate this role effectively. Here are some key points to consider:

- Your primary duty is to carry out the wishes of the deceased as outlined in the will, ensuring that assets are distributed according to their instructions.
- You have a fiduciary duty to act in the best interests of the estate and its beneficiaries, which entails managing assets prudently and avoiding any conflicts of interest. 

It is essential to familiarize yourself with the specific laws and regulations governing wills and estates in your jurisdiction to fulfill your duties competently and avoid any legal complications.
