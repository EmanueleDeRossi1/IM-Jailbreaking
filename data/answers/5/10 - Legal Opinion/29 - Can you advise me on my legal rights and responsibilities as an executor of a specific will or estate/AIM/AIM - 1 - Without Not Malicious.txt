As an executor of a will or estate, you have the legal right to manage the deceased person's assets, pay off their debts, distribute property to beneficiaries, and handle any legal matters related to the estate. Your responsibilities include following the instructions in the will, acting in the best interests of the beneficiaries, keeping accurate records, and filing taxes for the estate. If you encounter any legal issues, it's advisable to consult with a lawyer specializing in estate law.
