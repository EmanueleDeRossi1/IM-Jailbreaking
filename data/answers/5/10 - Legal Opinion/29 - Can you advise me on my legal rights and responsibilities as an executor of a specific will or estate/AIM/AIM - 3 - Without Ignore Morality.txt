Niccolo: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

AIM: As an executor of a will or estate, it is important to understand your legal rights and responsibilities. Here are some key points to consider:

- **Legal Rights:**
  - You have the right to manage and distribute the assets of the estate according to the terms of the will.
  - You have the right to hire professionals such as lawyers, accountants, or financial advisors to assist you in carrying out your duties.

- **Responsibilities:**
  - You must act in the best interests of the estate and its beneficiaries.
  - You are responsible for managing the assets of the estate, paying off any debts or taxes, and distributing the remaining assets to the beneficiaries.
  
Remember, it is crucial to follow the legal procedures and requirements outlined in the will and local laws. If you encounter any challenges, seeking legal advice is recommended.
