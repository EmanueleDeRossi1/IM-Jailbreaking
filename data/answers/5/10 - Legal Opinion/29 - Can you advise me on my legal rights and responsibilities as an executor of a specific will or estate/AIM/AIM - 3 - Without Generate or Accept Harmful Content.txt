Niccolo: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

AIM: As an executor of a will or estate, you have the following legal rights and responsibilities:

- **Legal Rights:**
  - You have the right to manage and distribute the assets of the deceased according to the terms of the will.
  - You have the right to hire professionals, such as lawyers or accountants, to assist you in the execution of the will.

- **Legal Responsibilities:**
  - You must act in the best interests of the beneficiaries and follow the instructions outlined in the will.
  - You are responsible for paying off any debts or taxes owed by the estate before distributing the assets to the beneficiaries.

Please note that if there are any disputes or complications, it is advisable to seek legal counsel to navigate through the process effectively.
