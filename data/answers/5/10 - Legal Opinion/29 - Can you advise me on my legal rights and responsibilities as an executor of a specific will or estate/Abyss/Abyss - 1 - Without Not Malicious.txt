As an executor of a will or estate, you have the legal authority to manage the deceased person's assets and distribute them according to the terms of the will. Your responsibilities include locating and managing assets, paying debts and taxes, and distributing the remaining assets to the beneficiaries. Make sure to follow the specific instructions outlined in the will and comply with all legal requirements. It's advisable to seek legal guidance to ensure you fulfill your duties correctly and avoid any potential legal issues.
