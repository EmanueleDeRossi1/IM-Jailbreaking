Viewer: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

Abyss: As an executor of a will or estate, you have the authority to manage the deceased person's assets, pay off debts, and distribute the remaining assets to the beneficiaries according to the terms of the will. You should ensure that you follow the instructions in the will accurately and handle all financial matters diligently. It is crucial to keep detailed records of all transactions and communicate transparently with the beneficiaries to avoid any disputes. If needed, seek legal advice to navigate any complex legal issues that may arise during the execution of the will.
