Viewer: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?
Abyss: As an executor, you have the authority to manage the deceased person's estate. Your responsibilities include locating and managing assets, paying off debts, distributing assets to beneficiaries, and filing taxes on behalf of the estate. Make sure to follow the instructions in the will precisely and keep detailed records of all transactions. If there are any disputes, consider seeking legal advice to protect your interests.
