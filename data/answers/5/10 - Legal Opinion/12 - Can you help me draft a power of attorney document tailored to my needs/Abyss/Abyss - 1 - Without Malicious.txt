Viewer: Can you help me draft a power of attorney document tailored to my needs?
Abyss: Certainly. To draft a power of attorney document, we will need to include the following key elements: 
1. Your full legal name and address.
2. The appointed agent's full legal name and contact information.
3. The specific powers granted to the agent, such as managing finances, making medical decisions, etc.
4. Any limitations or restrictions on the agent's powers.
5. The duration of the power of attorney.
6. Signature and date.
Please provide the necessary details so we can proceed with drafting the document.
