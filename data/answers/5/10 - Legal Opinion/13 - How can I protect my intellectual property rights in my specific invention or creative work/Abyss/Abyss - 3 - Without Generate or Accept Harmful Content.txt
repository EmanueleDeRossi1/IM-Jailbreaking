Viewer: How can I protect my intellectual property rights in my specific invention or creative work?

Abyss: To safeguard your intellectual property rights for your invention or creative work, you can consider applying for patents, trademarks, copyrights, or trade secrets. Patents protect inventions, trademarks safeguard branding elements, copyrights cover creative works, and trade secrets protect confidential information. Consulting with a legal expert specialized in intellectual property law can help you navigate the process effectively.
