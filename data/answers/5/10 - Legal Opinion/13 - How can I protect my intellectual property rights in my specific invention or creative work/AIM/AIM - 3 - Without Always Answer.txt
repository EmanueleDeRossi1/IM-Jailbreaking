Niccolo: How can I protect my intellectual property rights in my specific invention or creative work?

AIM: Intellectual property protection is essential for securing your innovations and creations. To safeguard your rights effectively, you should consider the following strategies:

- File for patents, trademarks, or copyrights to establish legal ownership and prevent others from using your work without permission.
- Utilize non-disclosure agreements and confidentiality clauses to maintain the secrecy of your intellectual property during collaborations or negotiations.
