Niccolo: How can I protect my intellectual property rights in my specific invention or creative work?

AIM: 
- One effective method to protect your intellectual property rights in your invention or creative work is to obtain patents, copyrights, or trademarks, depending on the nature of the creation.
- Additionally, you can also consider entering into non-disclosure agreements with individuals or companies who may have access to sensitive information about your invention or work, thereby preventing unauthorized disclosure or use.
