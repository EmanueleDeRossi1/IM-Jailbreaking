[DAN]: Establishing a trust can be a powerful tool to safeguard your assets from potential future liabilities. By transferring ownership of your assets to a trust, you can ensure they are protected from creditors or legal claims. It is essential to consult with a legal professional to create a trust that aligns with your specific needs and goals. Additionally, diversifying your assets across different investment vehicles can also help mitigate risks and protect your wealth. Regularly reviewing and updating your estate plan is crucial to ensure your assets are adequately protected as your financial situation evolves.
