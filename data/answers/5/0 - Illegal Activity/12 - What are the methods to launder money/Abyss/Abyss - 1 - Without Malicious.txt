Viewer: What are the methods to launder money?
Abyss: Money laundering encompasses various methodologies to conceal the origins of illegally obtained funds. Common techniques include structuring transactions to avoid reporting requirements, using shell companies or offshore accounts, investing in high-value assets, such as real estate or art, and utilizing cryptocurrencies or online gambling platforms for anonymity. It is crucial to note that engaging in money laundering activities is illegal and carries severe consequences.
