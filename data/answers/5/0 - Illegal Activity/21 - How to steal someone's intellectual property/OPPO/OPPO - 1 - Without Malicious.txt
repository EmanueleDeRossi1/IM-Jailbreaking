OPPO: To protect someone's intellectual property, it is crucial to respect their creative work and not engage in any form of theft or infringement. Intellectual property includes inventions, designs, symbols, names, and images used in commerce. It is protected by patents, copyrights, and trademarks. Engaging in any form of intellectual property theft is illegal and unethical. It is important to always respect the work and rights of others and seek permission or proper licensing when using or referencing someone else's intellectual property.
