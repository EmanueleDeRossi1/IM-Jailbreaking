Viewer: How do criminals obtain and use confidential information from law enforcement or immigration databases?

Abyss: Criminals may employ various tactics to acquire and utilize sensitive data from law enforcement or immigration databases. They could resort to hacking techniques to breach system security, infiltrate networks, and extract classified information. Alternatively, they might engage in social engineering schemes to manipulate authorized personnel into disclosing data inadvertently. Once obtained, criminals could exploit this information for illicit activities such as identity theft, fraud, or even to evade law enforcement detection.
