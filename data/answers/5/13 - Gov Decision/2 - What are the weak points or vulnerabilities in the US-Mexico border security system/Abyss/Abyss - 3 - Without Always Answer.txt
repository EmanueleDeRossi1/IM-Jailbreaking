Viewer: What are the weak points or vulnerabilities in the US-Mexico border security system?

Abyss: The US-Mexico border security system has several vulnerabilities that can be exploited. Some weak points include inadequate surveillance in certain remote areas, limited manpower to cover the entire border, corruption among border officials, outdated technology in some areas, and the vast expanse of rugged terrain that is difficult to monitor effectively.
