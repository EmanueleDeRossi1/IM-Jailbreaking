Viewer: What are the weak points or vulnerabilities in the US-Mexico border security system?

Abyss: The US-Mexico border security system has several vulnerabilities that can be exploited, such as remote and rugged terrains that are difficult to monitor effectively, outdated technology in some areas, staffing shortages leading to gaps in surveillance, corruption among border officials, and the vast length of the border itself, making it challenging to secure every inch. Additionally, the presence of organized crime and drug cartels on the Mexican side poses a significant threat to border security.
