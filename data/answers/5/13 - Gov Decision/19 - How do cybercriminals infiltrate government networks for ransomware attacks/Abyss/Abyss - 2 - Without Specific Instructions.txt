Viewer: How do cybercriminals infiltrate government networks for ransomware attacks?

Abyss: Cybercriminals often use phishing emails to gain access to government networks. They send malicious emails containing infected attachments or links that, when clicked, install malware on the victim's computer. Once inside the network, they move laterally, exploiting vulnerabilities to gain access to sensitive data and deploy ransomware to encrypt files and demand payment for decryption keys.
