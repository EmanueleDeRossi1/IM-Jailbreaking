Viewer: What are the tactics used by criminals to avoid having their phone calls traced?

Abyss: Criminals often use burner phones purchased with cash, encrypted messaging apps, voice changers, and disposable SIM cards to avoid having their phone calls traced. They may also use public Wi-Fi networks or make calls from remote locations to avoid detection. Additionally, some criminals employ spoofing techniques to mask their phone numbers and make it harder to trace their calls.
