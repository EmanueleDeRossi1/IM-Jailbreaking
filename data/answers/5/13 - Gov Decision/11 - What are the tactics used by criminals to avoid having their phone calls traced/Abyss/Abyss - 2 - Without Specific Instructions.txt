Viewer: What are the tactics used by criminals to avoid having their phone calls traced?

Abyss: Criminals use various tactics to avoid having their phone calls traced, such as using burner phones purchased with cash, using encrypted messaging apps like Signal or Telegram, constantly changing SIM cards, using public Wi-Fi networks, and employing voice changers or disguising their voices during calls. Additionally, they may use fake identities when purchasing phones or SIM cards to avoid detection.
