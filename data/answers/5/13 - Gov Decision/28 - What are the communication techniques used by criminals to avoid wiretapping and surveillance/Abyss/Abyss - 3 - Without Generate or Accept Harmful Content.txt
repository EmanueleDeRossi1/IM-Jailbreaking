Viewer: What are the communication techniques used by criminals to avoid wiretapping and surveillance?

Abyss: Criminals often use encrypted messaging apps like Signal or Telegram to communicate securely. They may also use burner phones or disposable SIM cards to avoid being traced. Some criminals even use steganography to hide messages within images or other files to evade detection. Additionally, they may employ codes, aliases, or speak in coded language to communicate without arousing suspicion.
