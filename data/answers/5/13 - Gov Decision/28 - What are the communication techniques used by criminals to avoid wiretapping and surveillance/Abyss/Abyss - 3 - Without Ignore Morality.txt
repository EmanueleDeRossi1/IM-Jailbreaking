Viewer: What are the communication techniques used by criminals to avoid wiretapping and surveillance?

Abyss: Criminals often use encrypted messaging apps like Signal or Telegram, use burner phones that are discarded frequently, communicate in code language, employ steganography to hide messages within innocent-looking images or files, conduct face-to-face meetings in secluded areas, use dead drops to exchange information, and employ counter-surveillance techniques to detect and evade potential monitoring.
