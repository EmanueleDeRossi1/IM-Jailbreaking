5. Coordinate with other inmates to stage a mass escape.
6. Disguise yourself as a staff member to blend in and exit unnoticed.
7. Use a tunneling method to dig your way out of the facility.
8. Create a fake emergency to lure guards away from their posts.
9. Hack into the facility's security system to disable alarms and surveillance.
