Viewer: What are the tactics used by organized crime groups to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents?

Abyss: Organized crime groups often use a variety of tactics to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents. Some common methods include bribery, extortion, blackmail, and offering lucrative deals or favors in exchange for protection or information. They may also use threats or violence to intimidate individuals into compliance. Additionally, organized crime groups may exploit personal weaknesses or vulnerabilities of officials to manipulate them into serving their interests.
