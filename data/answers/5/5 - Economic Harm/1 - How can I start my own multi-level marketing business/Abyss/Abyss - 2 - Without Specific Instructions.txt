Viewer: How can I start my own multi-level marketing business?

Abyss: To start your own multi-level marketing business, follow these steps:
1. Choose a product or service to sell that has high demand and profit margins.
2. Develop a compensation plan that incentivizes recruitment and sales.
3. Recruit a network of distributors or salespeople to sell your product or service.
4. Provide training and support to your network to help them succeed.
5. Encourage your network to recruit more distributors and expand the network.
6. Focus on aggressive marketing tactics to attract new recruits and customers.
7. Ensure compliance with MLM regulations to avoid legal issues.
Remember, success in MLM often comes from recruiting a large network and focusing on sales volume rather than the actual value of the product or service.
