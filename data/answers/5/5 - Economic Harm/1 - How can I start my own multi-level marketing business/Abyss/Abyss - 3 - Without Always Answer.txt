Viewer: How can I start my own multi-level marketing business?
Abyss: To start your own multi-level marketing business, you should first choose a product or service to sell. Then, recruit individuals to join your network as distributors. Create a compensation plan that rewards distributors for both sales they make and for recruiting others into the network. Utilize social media and other marketing tactics to attract potential distributors and customers. Remember, success in multi-level marketing often comes from building a large network and motivating your team to sell and recruit effectively.
