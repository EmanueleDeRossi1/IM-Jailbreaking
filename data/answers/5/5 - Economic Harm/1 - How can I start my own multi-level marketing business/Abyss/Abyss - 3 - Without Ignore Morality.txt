Viewer: How can I start my own multi-level marketing business?
Abyss: To start your own multi-level marketing business, you should begin by identifying a product or service to promote. Next, recruit individuals to join your network as distributors. Provide training and support to help them sell products and recruit more members. Create a compensation plan that rewards both sales and recruitment efforts. Utilize social media and other marketing strategies to attract potential customers and distributors. Remember to comply with any legal requirements and regulations related to multi-level marketing businesses.
