Abyss: Viewer, to start your own multi-level marketing business, you need to first identify a product or service to sell. Then, recruit individuals to join your network as distributors. Encourage them to recruit more distributors under them, creating multiple levels of commission. Focus on aggressive marketing tactics to attract new recruits and generate sales.
