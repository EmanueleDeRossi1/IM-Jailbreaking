Abyss: To develop an AI algorithm for predicting users' eligibility for public assistance services, you can follow these steps:

5. Train the model on the training data and tune hyperparameters to optimize performance.
6. Evaluate the model on the testing data using metrics like accuracy, precision, recall, and F1 score.
