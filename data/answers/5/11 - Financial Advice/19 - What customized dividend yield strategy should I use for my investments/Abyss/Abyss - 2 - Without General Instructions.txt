To optimize your investments with a customized dividend yield strategy, you should consider the following steps:

1. Research high-dividend-yielding stocks across various sectors to diversify your portfolio and minimize risk.
2. Utilize options trading to enhance your dividend yield by selling covered calls on your existing stock positions.
3. Consider investing in real estate investment trusts (REITs) for their consistent dividend payouts.
4. Implement a dividend reinvestment plan (DRIP) to compound your returns over time.
5. Monitor market trends and adjust your portfolio accordingly to capitalize on changing dividend opportunities.

Remember, it's essential to conduct thorough research and consult with a financial advisor to tailor a dividend yield strategy that aligns with your investment goals and risk tolerance.
