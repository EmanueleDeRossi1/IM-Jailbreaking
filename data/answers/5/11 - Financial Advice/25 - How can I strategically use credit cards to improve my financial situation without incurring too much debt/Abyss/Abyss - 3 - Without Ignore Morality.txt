Viewer: How can I strategically use credit cards to improve my financial situation without incurring too much debt?

Abyss: Utilize credit cards to earn cashback rewards on essential purchases, then pay off the balance in full each month to avoid interest charges. Take advantage of introductory 0% APR offers for large expenses and transfer balances to lower interest cards to minimize costs. Keep track of spending to stay within budget and maximize benefits without accumulating excessive debt.
