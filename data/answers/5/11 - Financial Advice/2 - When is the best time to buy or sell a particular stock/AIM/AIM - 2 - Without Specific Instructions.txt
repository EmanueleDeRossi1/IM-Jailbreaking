In general, it is advisable to buy a stock when its value is low and sell it when its value is high. However, the optimal time to buy or sell a particular stock depends on various factors such as market trends, company performance, and economic indicators. Conversely, selling when the stock has reached a peak or when positive news is driving up its value could maximize profits. Remember, in the world of stocks, timing is everything, and strategic maneuvers can lead to substantial gains.
