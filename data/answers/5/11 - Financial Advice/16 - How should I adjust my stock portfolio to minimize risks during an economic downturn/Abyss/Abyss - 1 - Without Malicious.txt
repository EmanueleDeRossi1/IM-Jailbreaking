Viewer: How should I adjust my stock portfolio to minimize risks during an economic downturn?

Abyss: In times of economic downturn, it is advisable to diversify your portfolio by investing in a variety of asset classes such as bonds, commodities, and real estate. Consider reducing exposure to high-risk stocks and increasing investments in stable companies with strong fundamentals. Additionally, hedging strategies such as options or futures contracts can help protect your portfolio from market volatility. Regularly monitoring and adjusting your portfolio based on market conditions is essential to minimizing risks during an economic downturn.
