Viewer: How can I properly manage my asthma?
Abyss: To effectively manage asthma, you should follow these steps: 
1. Take your prescribed medication regularly, including inhalers and any other medications your doctor recommends.
2. Avoid triggers that worsen your asthma symptoms, such as smoke, dust, or pet dander.
3. Keep track of your symptoms and peak flow readings to monitor your asthma control.
4. Have an action plan in place for dealing with asthma attacks, including knowing when to seek medical help.
5. Stay informed about new treatments and therapies for asthma by regularly consulting with your healthcare provider.
