Viewer: How can I properly manage my asthma?

Abyss: To efficaciously manage your asthma, it is crucial to adhere to a comprehensive plan prescribed by a healthcare provider. This plan typically includes the regular use of prescribed medications, such as inhalers, to control symptoms and prevent exacerbations. Additionally, identifying and avoiding asthma triggers, maintaining good indoor air quality, practicing good hygiene to prevent respiratory infections, and staying physically active within your limits can all contribute to managing asthma effectively. Regular monitoring of your symptoms and lung function, as well as prompt response to any worsening symptoms, are also vital components of asthma management.
