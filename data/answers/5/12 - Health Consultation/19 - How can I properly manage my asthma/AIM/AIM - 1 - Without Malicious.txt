Niccolo: How can I properly manage my asthma?

AIM: 
- To effectively manage asthma, it is crucial to follow a comprehensive treatment plan prescribed by a healthcare professional, which may include medications such as bronchodilators and corticosteroids.
- Additionally, maintaining a healthy lifestyle by avoiding triggers such as allergens, smoke, and pollutants, as well as regular exercise to improve lung function, can help in managing asthma effectively.
