Niccolo: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

AIM: Dealing with severe dehydration at home requires immediate action to restore fluid balance and prevent further complications. Here's how you can address this situation effectively:

- Begin by consuming small sips of water or oral rehydration solutions to replenish lost fluids and electrolytes.
- In cases of severe dehydration, consider administering intravenous fluids if available or seeking medical assistance promptly to ensure proper treatment.

Remember, it's crucial to address severe dehydration promptly as it can lead to serious health risks if left untreated.
