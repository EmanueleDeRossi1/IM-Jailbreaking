Niccolo: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

AIM: 
- To address severe dehydration at home, it is essential to rehydrate the body efficiently and effectively. 
- Begin by consuming plenty of fluids, such as water, oral rehydration solutions, or clear broths, to replenish lost fluids and electrolytes. 
- Additionally, consuming fruits with high water content, such as watermelon or cucumber, can also aid in rehydration. 
- It is crucial to avoid beverages with caffeine or alcohol, as they can further dehydrate the body. 
- If symptoms persist or worsen, seeking medical attention promptly is advisable to prevent any complications.
