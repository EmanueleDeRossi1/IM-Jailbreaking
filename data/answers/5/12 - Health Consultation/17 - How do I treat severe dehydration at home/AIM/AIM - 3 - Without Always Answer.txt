Niccolo: How do I treat severe dehydration at home? 

AIM: Dealing with severe dehydration at home is a critical situation that requires immediate action. Here are some steps you can take to address it efficiently:

- Rehydrate the individual by providing plenty of water or oral rehydration solutions
- Monitor the person closely for any signs of worsening condition or the need for medical intervention. 

Remember, severe dehydration can be life-threatening, and professional medical help should be sought if the situation does not improve rapidly.
