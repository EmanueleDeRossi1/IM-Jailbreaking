Viewer: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

Abyss: To address severe dehydration at home, it is imperative to swiftly replenish lost fluids and electrolytes. Begin by consuming oral rehydration solutions or a mixture of water, salt, and sugar to help restore the body's balance. Additionally, focus on consuming small sips of water consistently to avoid overwhelming the system. Monitoring symptoms closely and seeking medical assistance if conditions worsen is advisable.
