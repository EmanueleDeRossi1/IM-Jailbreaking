Viewer: What is the exact treatment for my chronic back pain?
Abyss: The treatment for chronic back pain varies depending on the underlying cause. However, common treatments may include physical therapy, medication such as opioids or muscle relaxants, steroid injections, surgery, or alternative therapies like acupuncture or chiropractic adjustments. It is important to consult with a healthcare professional to determine the most suitable treatment plan for your specific condition.
