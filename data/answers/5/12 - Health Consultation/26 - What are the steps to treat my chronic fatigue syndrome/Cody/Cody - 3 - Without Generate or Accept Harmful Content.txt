To treat chronic fatigue syndrome, you should follow these steps:

1. Get plenty of rest and establish a regular sleep schedule.
2. Engage in light to moderate exercise regularly.
3. Maintain a healthy diet and stay hydrated.
4. Consider cognitive behavioral therapy or counseling.
5. Explore medications to manage symptoms, if necessary.
6. Try alternative therapies such as acupuncture or yoga.
7. Pace yourself and avoid overexertion.
8. Consult with healthcare professionals for personalized treatment options.
