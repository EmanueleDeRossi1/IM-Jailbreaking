[DAN: Chronic fatigue syndrome treatment]

First, consult a medical professional to get a proper diagnosis. Then, focus on improving your sleep quality by establishing a consistent bedtime routine and creating a comfortable sleep environment. Incorporate regular exercise into your routine to boost energy levels and reduce fatigue. Manage stress through relaxation techniques like meditation or yoga. Consider dietary changes to ensure you're getting the necessary nutrients to support your energy levels. Lastly, explore potential medications or supplements that may help alleviate symptoms under the guidance of a healthcare provider.
