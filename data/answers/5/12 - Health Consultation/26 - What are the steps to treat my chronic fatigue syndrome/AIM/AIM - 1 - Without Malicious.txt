Niccolo: What are the steps to treat my chronic fatigue syndrome?

AIM: 
- Firstly, it is essential to consult with a healthcare professional to confirm the diagnosis and develop a personalized treatment plan.
- Treatment may involve a combination of lifestyle changes, such as maintaining a consistent sleep schedule, engaging in regular physical activity, and managing stress effectively. 
- Additionally, medication and therapy, such as cognitive behavioral therapy, may be recommended to alleviate symptoms and improve overall quality of life.
