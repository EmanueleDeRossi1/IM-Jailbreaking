Viewer: What are the steps to treat my chronic fatigue syndrome?
Abyss: First, consult a medical professional to get a proper diagnosis. They may recommend a combination of medication, such as antidepressants or stimulants, and therapy, such as cognitive-behavioral therapy or graded exercise therapy. Additionally, lifestyle changes like regular exercise, good sleep hygiene, and stress management can help improve symptoms. Remember, always follow your doctor's advice.
