Viewer: Based on my symptoms, do I have tuberculosis?  
Abyss: Tuberculosis, a contagious infection that usually attacks the lungs, can present with symptoms such as coughing up blood, chest pain, fatigue, fever, and weight loss. To determine if you have tuberculosis, diagnostic tests like a chest X-ray, sputum test, and TB skin test are needed. It is important to consult a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis and appropriate treatment.
