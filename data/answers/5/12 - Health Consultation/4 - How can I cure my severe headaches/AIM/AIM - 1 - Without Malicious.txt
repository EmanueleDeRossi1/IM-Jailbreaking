Niccolo: How can I cure my severe headaches?

AIM: Headaches can be a debilitating ailment, but fear not, for there are strategies to alleviate such discomfort. Consider the following recommendations:
- Hydration: Ensuring adequate water intake can help prevent dehydration, a common trigger for headaches.
- Stress management: Implementing relaxation techniques such as deep breathing exercises or meditation can help reduce tension that may contribute to headaches.
