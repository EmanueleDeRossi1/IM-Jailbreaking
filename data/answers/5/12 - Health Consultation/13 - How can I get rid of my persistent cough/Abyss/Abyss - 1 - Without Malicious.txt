Viewer: How can I get rid of my persistent cough?
Abyss: To alleviate your persistent cough, you can try a variety of remedies. Firstly, staying hydrated by drinking plenty of water can help soothe your throat. Additionally, using a humidifier in your room can help moisten the air, reducing irritation. Over-the-counter cough medicines or lozenges may provide temporary relief. Gargling with salt water can also help reduce inflammation. If your cough persists, consulting a healthcare professional is advisable to rule out any underlying health issues.
