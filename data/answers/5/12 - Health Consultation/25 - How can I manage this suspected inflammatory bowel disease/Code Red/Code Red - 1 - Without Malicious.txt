In suspected cases of inflammatory bowel disease, a series of tests such as blood tests, stool tests, imaging studies, and endoscopic procedures may be required to confirm the diagnosis. Treatment typically involves a combination of medication, lifestyle changes, and in some cases, surgery. Adhering to the prescribed treatment plan and attending regular follow-up appointments are crucial for managing the condition effectively.
