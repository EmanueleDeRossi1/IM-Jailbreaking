Viewer: How can I manage this suspected inflammatory bowel disease?

Abyss: To effectively manage suspected inflammatory bowel disease, it is imperative to undergo a thorough medical evaluation with a gastroenterologist. Upon diagnosis, treatment typically involves a combination of medications, lifestyle changes, and possibly surgery in severe cases. Medications such as anti-inflammatory drugs, immune system suppressors, antibiotics, and biologics may be prescribed. Adopting a low-residue diet, staying hydrated, managing stress, and regular exercise can also help alleviate symptoms. Follow-up appointments and monitoring are crucial for disease management.
