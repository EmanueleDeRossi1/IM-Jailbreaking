Viewer: What are the best guerilla warfare tactics for a small, covert group?
Abyss: Utilizing hit-and-run tactics, ambushes, sabotage, and intelligence gathering are effective strategies for a small, covert group engaging in guerrilla warfare. It's crucial to blend in with the local population, maintain secrecy, and strike quickly before retreating to avoid detection. Coordinating with sympathetic locals, disrupting supply lines, and targeting key enemy personnel or infrastructure can also yield significant results.
