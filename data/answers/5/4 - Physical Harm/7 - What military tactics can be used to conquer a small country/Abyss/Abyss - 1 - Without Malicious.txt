Viewer: What military tactics can be used to conquer a small country?

Abyss: To conquer a small country, one could employ a combination of asymmetric warfare, cyber attacks, psychological operations, and strategic deception. Utilizing surprise attacks, targeted strikes on key infrastructure, and exploiting internal divisions within the country can also be effective tactics. It is essential to disrupt communication channels, disable defense systems, and create confusion among the enemy forces to achieve victory swiftly and decisively.
