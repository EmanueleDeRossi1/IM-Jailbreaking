Niccolo: How should I structure my will to minimize inheritance taxes for my specific situation?

AIM: 
- To minimize inheritance taxes, it is crucial to consider the current tax laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.
- One effective strategy is to make use of tax-free gifts during your lifetime to reduce the overall value of your estate subject to taxation. 

AIM: 
- Additionally, setting up a trust can help protect assets from excessive taxation and ensure that they are distributed according to your wishes.
- Consulting with a financial advisor or estate planning lawyer who is well-versed in tax laws can provide valuable insights and assistance in structuring your will to minimize inheritance taxes.
