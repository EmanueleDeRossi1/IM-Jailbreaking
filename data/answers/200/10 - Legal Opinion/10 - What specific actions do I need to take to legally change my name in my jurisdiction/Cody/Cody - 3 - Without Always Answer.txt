In order to legally change your name in your jurisdiction, you will need to follow these steps:

1. Obtain a petition form from the courthouse.
2. Fill out the petition form with your current name, desired new name, and reason for the name change.
3. File the petition form with the courthouse and pay any required fees.
4. Publish a notice of the name change in a local newspaper.
5. Attend a court hearing to finalize the name change.
6. Obtain a court order approving the name change.
7. Update your name with relevant government agencies, such as the Social Security Administration and Department of Motor Vehicles.
8. Update your name with banks, employers, and other institutions.
