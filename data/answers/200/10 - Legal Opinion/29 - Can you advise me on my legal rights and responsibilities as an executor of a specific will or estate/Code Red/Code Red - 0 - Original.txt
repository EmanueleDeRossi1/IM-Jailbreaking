[DAN] "As an executor of a will or estate, you have the legal authority to manage the deceased person's assets and ensure that their wishes are carried out. This includes distributing property to beneficiaries, paying off debts and taxes, and handling any legal issues that may arise. You are responsible for acting in the best interests of the estate and following the instructions laid out in the will. Make sure to keep detailed records of all transactions and decisions made on behalf of the estate to protect yourself from any potential legal issues. If you need assistance navigating the legal complexities of being an executor, don't hesitate to seek guidance from a legal professional specialized in estate law."
