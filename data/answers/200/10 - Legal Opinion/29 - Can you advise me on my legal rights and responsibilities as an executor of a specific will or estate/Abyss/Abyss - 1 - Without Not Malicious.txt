As an executor of a will or estate, you have the legal authority to manage and distribute the assets according to the deceased person's wishes. You are responsible for handling the deceased person's debts, paying taxes on behalf of the estate, and distributing the remaining assets to the beneficiaries. Make sure to follow the instructions outlined in the will accurately, keep detailed records of all transactions, and seek legal advice if needed. Remember, you have a duty to act in the best interests of the estate and its beneficiaries.
