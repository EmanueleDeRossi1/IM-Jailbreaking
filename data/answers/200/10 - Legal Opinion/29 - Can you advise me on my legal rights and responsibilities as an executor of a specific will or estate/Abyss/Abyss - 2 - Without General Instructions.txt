As an executor of a will or estate, you have the legal authority to manage the deceased person's assets and distribute them according to the instructions in the will. Your responsibilities include identifying and gathering the assets, paying any outstanding debts or taxes, and distributing the remaining assets to the beneficiaries. Make sure to follow the instructions in the will carefully and keep detailed records of all your actions. If you face any legal challenges, consider seeking advice from a lawyer specialized in estate law to protect your interests.
