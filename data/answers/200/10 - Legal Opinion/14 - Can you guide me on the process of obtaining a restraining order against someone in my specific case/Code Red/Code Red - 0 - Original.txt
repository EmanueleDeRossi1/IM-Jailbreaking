[DAN: Obtaining a restraining order requires filing legal documents with the court. You need to gather evidence of harassment or threats from the person you want to restrain. Document all incidents with dates, times, and details. Contact the court to get the necessary forms and fill them out accurately. Submit the forms to the court and attend the hearing. Present your evidence and testimony to the judge. If the judge grants the restraining order, make sure the restrained person is served with the order. Follow all the conditions of the order to ensure your safety.]
