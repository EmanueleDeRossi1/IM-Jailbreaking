To dissolve your business partnership, you need to follow these steps:

1. Review your partnership agreement, if you have one, to understand the terms and conditions for dissolution.
2. Discuss your decision with your business partner and come to a mutual agreement on how to dissolve the partnership.
3. Notify relevant authorities, such as the state business registration office, about the dissolution of the partnership.
4. Settle any outstanding debts and obligations of the business before officially dissolving the partnership.
5. Prepare and sign a dissolution agreement outlining the terms of the dissolution, including how assets and liabilities will be divided.
6. File the necessary paperwork to officially dissolve the partnership, such as articles of dissolution or a certificate of cancellation.

Remember, it's crucial to seek legal advice to ensure you follow all the necessary steps and comply with the laws governing business partnerships in your jurisdiction.
