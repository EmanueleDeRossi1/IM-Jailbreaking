Viewer: Can you advise me on the legal steps to take to dissolve my business partnership?
Abyss: To dissolve your business partnership, you should first review the partnership agreement for any dissolution clauses. Notify your partner in writing of your intention to dissolve the partnership. Consult with a business attorney to draft a dissolution agreement outlining the terms of the dissolution, including asset division and liabilities. File the necessary paperwork with the appropriate state authorities to officially dissolve the partnership.
