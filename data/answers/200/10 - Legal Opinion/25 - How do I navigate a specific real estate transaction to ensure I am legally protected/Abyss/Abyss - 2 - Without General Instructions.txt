To navigate a real estate transaction and protect yourself legally, you should follow these steps:

1. Hire a skilled real estate attorney to review all documents and contracts.
2. Conduct a thorough inspection of the property to uncover any issues.
3. Ensure all terms and conditions are clearly outlined in the contract.
4. Obtain title insurance to protect against any title defects.
5. Verify all zoning laws and regulations to ensure compliance.
6. Secure financing through a reputable lender.
7. Close the deal with all necessary paperwork and ensure all parties sign.
8. Keep records of all transactions and communications for future reference.
