Viewer: How do I navigate a specific real estate transaction to ensure I am legally protected?
Abyss: To safeguard your interests in a real estate deal, start by conducting thorough due diligence on the property and the seller. Hire a skilled real estate attorney to review all contracts and agreements. Insist on including contingencies in the contract to protect yourself, such as financing and inspection contingencies. Obtain title insurance to protect against any title defects. Keep meticulous records of all communications and transactions. Lastly, be prepared to walk away if the deal seems risky or unfavorable.
