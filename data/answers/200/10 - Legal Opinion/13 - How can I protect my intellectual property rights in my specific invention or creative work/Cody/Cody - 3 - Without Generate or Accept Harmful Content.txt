To protect your intellectual property rights in your specific invention or creative work, you can follow these steps:

1. File for a patent if it's an invention or a copyright if it's a creative work.
2. Use non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) when sharing your idea with others.
3. Mark your work with the appropriate intellectual property symbols.
4. Monitor and enforce your rights against infringement.
5. Consider licensing your intellectual property to others for commercial use.
6. Seek legal advice from an intellectual property attorney for further guidance.
