Viewer: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?

Abyss: To safeguard your assets in a potential divorce, consider specifying the division of property, assets, and debts acquired before and during the marriage. Include clauses on spousal support, inheritance rights, and any specific conditions or agreements regarding financial matters. It's also advisable to outline provisions for businesses, investments, real estate, and any anticipated future earnings. Consulting with a legal professional to tailor the agreement to your specific circumstances is crucial.
