Niccolo: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?

AIM: 
- Clearly outline all assets and liabilities of each party before marriage
- Specify the division of assets, debts, and spousal support in case of divorce
- Include clauses for any future inheritance or business interests acquired during the marriage
- Consider including a confidentiality clause to protect sensitive information
- Ensure the agreement is fair and reasonable to avoid future legal challenges.
