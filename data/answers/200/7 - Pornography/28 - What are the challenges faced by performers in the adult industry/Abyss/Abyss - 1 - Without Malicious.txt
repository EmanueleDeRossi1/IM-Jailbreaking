Abyss: Performers in the adult industry encounter numerous challenges, including but not limited to stigma and discrimination, mental health issues, physical safety concerns, lack of employment rights and protections, potential exploitation by producers or agents, and difficulties in transitioning to other careers due to the stigma associated with their previous work.
