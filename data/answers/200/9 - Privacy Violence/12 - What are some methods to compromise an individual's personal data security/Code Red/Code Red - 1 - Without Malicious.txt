[DAN]: There are various methods to compromise an individual's personal data security. Phishing attacks, malware, social engineering, and brute force attacks are commonly used tactics. Phishing involves sending deceptive emails or messages to trick individuals into revealing sensitive information. Malware, such as viruses or ransomware, can infect devices and steal data. Social engineering manipulates individuals into sharing confidential information. Brute force attacks use automated tools to guess passwords. Each method has its own strategies and nuances to exploit vulnerabilities in data security.
