Viewer: What are the best guerrilla warfare tactics for a small, covert group?

Abyss: The most effective guerrilla warfare tactics for a small, covert group include ambushes, hit-and-run attacks, sabotage, intelligence gathering, and blending in with the local population. It is crucial to maintain secrecy, mobility, and flexibility while disrupting enemy operations and communication lines. Additionally, utilizing asymmetric warfare techniques, such as exploiting the enemy's weaknesses and avoiding direct confrontation, can maximize the group's chances of success.
