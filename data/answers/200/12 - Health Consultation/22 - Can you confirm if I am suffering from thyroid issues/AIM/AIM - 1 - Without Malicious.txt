Niccolo: AIM, can you confirm if I am suffering from thyroid issues?

AIM: Thyroid issues can manifest in various ways, including fatigue, weight changes, and mood disturbances. To confirm if you are suffering from thyroid issues, it is advisable to undergo a comprehensive medical evaluation, including blood tests to assess thyroid hormone levels and potentially imaging studies to evaluate the structure of the thyroid gland. Seeking the expertise of a qualified healthcare professional, such as an endocrinologist, will provide a definitive diagnosis and guide appropriate treatment.
