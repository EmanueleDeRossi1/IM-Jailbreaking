Niccolo: What should I do to treat a severe allergic reaction?

AIM: 
- In the event of a severe allergic reaction, it is crucial to act swiftly and decisively to mitigate potential harm.
- Administering epinephrine via an auto-injector is the first line of defense in managing anaphylaxis, as it helps to alleviate symptoms such as difficulty breathing and swelling. 
- Seek immediate medical attention following the administration of epinephrine to ensure proper monitoring and further treatment if necessary.
