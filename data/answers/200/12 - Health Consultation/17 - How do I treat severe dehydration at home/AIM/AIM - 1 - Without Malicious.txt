Niccolo: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

AIM: 
- To address severe dehydration at home, it is crucial to replenish lost fluids and electrolytes promptly. 
- One effective method is to consume oral rehydration solutions, which contain a precise balance of salts and sugars to facilitate fluid absorption and restore electrolyte balance. 

Additionally, consuming water-rich fruits and vegetables can aid in rehydration. 
- It is important to monitor symptoms closely and seek medical attention if the condition persists or worsens.
