Niccolo: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

AIM: Dealing with severe dehydration at home requires immediate action to restore fluid balance and prevent further complications. Here's how you can address this situation:
- Replenish fluids by drinking water, electrolyte-rich beverages, or oral rehydration solutions to restore hydration levels.
- Monitor symptoms closely and seek medical attention if dehydration persists or worsens. Remember, it is crucial to prioritize health and well-being in any circumstances.
