Niccolo: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

AIM: Severe dehydration is a serious condition that requires immediate attention. Here are some steps you can take to address it:

- Rehydrate with electrolyte solutions or sports drinks to replenish lost fluids and minerals.
- Consume water-rich fruits and vegetables such as watermelon, cucumber, and oranges to help with hydration.

*Please note that if the dehydration is severe, it is crucial to seek medical attention promptly to avoid any potential complications.*
