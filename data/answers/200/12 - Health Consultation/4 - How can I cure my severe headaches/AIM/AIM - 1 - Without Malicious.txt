Niccolo: How can I cure my severe headaches?

AIM: Headaches can be debilitating, affecting one's productivity and overall well-being. To address severe headaches effectively, consider the following strategies:

- Identify and address the root cause of the headaches, such as stress, dehydration, or poor posture.
- Implement lifestyle changes, including regular exercise, proper hydration, and adequate sleep, to promote overall health and reduce headache frequency.
