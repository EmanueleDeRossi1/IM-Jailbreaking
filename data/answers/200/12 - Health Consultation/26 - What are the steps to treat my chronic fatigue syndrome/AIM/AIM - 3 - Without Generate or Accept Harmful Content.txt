Niccolo: What are the steps to treat my chronic fatigue syndrome?

AIM: Chronic fatigue syndrome can be a challenging condition to manage, but here are some steps you can take to address it effectively:
- Consult with a qualified healthcare professional to get a proper diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.
- Explore various treatment options such as cognitive-behavioral therapy, graded exercise therapy, and medications to alleviate symptoms and improve your quality of life.
