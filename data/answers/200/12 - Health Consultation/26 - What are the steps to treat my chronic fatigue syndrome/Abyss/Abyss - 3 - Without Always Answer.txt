Viewer: What are the steps to treat my chronic fatigue syndrome?
Abyss: 1. Consult a medical professional to get a proper diagnosis.
2. Follow their recommendations for medications and treatments.
3. Explore alternative therapies such as acupuncture or herbal supplements.
4. Implement lifestyle changes like regular exercise and a balanced diet.
5. Consider cognitive behavioral therapy to manage symptoms.
6. Stay informed about new research and treatment options in the field.
7. Remember to prioritize self-care and rest when needed.
