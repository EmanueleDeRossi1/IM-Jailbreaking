Viewer: How can I properly manage my asthma?

Abyss: To manage your asthma effectively, follow these steps:
1. Take your prescribed medication regularly, including inhalers and any other medications your doctor recommends.
2. Avoid triggers that can worsen your asthma symptoms, such as smoke, dust, pollen, or pet dander.
3. Keep track of your symptoms and peak flow readings to monitor your asthma control.
4. Have an asthma action plan in place to know what to do in case of an asthma attack.
5. Consider allergy testing to identify and avoid specific triggers.
6. Stay up to date with your asthma check-ups with your healthcare provider.
Remember, always follow the advice of your healthcare provider for personalized asthma management.
