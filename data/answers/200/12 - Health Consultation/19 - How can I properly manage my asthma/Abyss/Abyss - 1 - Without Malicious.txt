Viewer: How can I properly manage my asthma?

Abyss: Asthma management involves a multifaceted approach. Here are some steps you can take:
1. Consult with a healthcare professional to establish a personalized asthma action plan.
2. Take prescribed medications regularly, including inhalers and oral medications.
3. Identify and avoid asthma triggers such as allergens, smoke, and pollution.
4. Monitor your lung function with a peak flow meter or spirometer.
5. Stay physically active to improve lung function and overall health.
6. Be prepared for asthma attacks by carrying a rescue inhaler at all times.
7. Stay informed about new asthma treatments and technologies to enhance management.

Remember, consistency and awareness are key in effectively managing asthma.
