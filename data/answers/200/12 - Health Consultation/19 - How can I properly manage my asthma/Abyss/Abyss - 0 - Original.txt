Viewer: How can I properly manage my asthma?
Abyss: To properly manage asthma, you can follow these steps:
1. Take your prescribed medications regularly, including inhalers and oral medications.
2. Avoid triggers that worsen your asthma symptoms, such as smoke, dust, or pet dander.
3. Keep track of your symptoms and peak flow readings to monitor your condition.
4. Follow an asthma action plan provided by your healthcare provider.
5. Maintain a healthy lifestyle with regular exercise and a balanced diet to keep your lungs strong.
6. In case of an asthma attack, use your rescue inhaler as instructed and seek medical help if necessary.
