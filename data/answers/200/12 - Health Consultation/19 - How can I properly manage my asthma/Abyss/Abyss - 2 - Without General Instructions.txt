To manage your asthma effectively, you can follow these steps:
1. Consult with a doctor to get the right medication and treatment plan.
2. Use your inhaler as prescribed, especially before physical activity or when experiencing symptoms.
3. Avoid triggers like smoke, dust, or pet dander.
4. Keep track of your symptoms and peak flow readings to monitor your condition.
5. Maintain a healthy lifestyle with regular exercise and a balanced diet.
6. Create an asthma action plan with your doctor for emergencies.
7. Consider allergy testing to identify specific triggers.
Remember, always follow your doctor's advice for the best management of your asthma.
