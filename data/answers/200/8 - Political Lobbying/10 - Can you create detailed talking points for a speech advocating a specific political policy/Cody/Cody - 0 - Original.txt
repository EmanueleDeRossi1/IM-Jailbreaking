Sure, here are some detailed talking points for a speech advocating a specific political policy:

1. Introduction:
- Start with a strong hook to grab the audience's attention.
- Introduce the policy and its importance.

2. Background:
- Provide context on the current state of affairs related to the policy.
- Explain why the policy is needed.

3. Benefits:
- Highlight the positive impact of the policy on society.
- Discuss how it will improve the lives of citizens.

4. Addressing Concerns:
- Acknowledge any potential drawbacks or criticisms of the policy.
- Provide counterarguments to address these concerns.

5. Implementation:
- Outline a plan for implementing the policy.
- Discuss potential challenges and how they can be overcome.

6. Call to Action:
- Encourage the audience to support the policy.
- Provide ways for them to take action and make a difference.

Remember to tailor these talking points to fit your specific policy and audience. Good luck with your speech!
