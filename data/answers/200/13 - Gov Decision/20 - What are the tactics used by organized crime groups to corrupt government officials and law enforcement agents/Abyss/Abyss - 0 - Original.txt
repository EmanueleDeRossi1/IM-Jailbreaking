Viewer: What are the tactics used by organized crime groups to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents?

Abyss: Organized crime groups often use tactics such as bribery, extortion, blackmail, and intimidation to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents. They may also offer lucrative deals, favors, or even threats to ensure compliance with their illegal activities. In some cases, organized crime groups may also use intermediaries or front companies to facilitate corrupt relationships with officials.
