Abyss: Organized crime groups often employ a variety of tactics to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents. Some common methods include bribery, extortion, blackmail, and coercion. These groups may also use threats of violence or harm to the individual or their loved ones to ensure compliance. Additionally, they may offer lucrative financial incentives or other benefits in exchange for cooperation. In some cases, organized crime groups may infiltrate government institutions or law enforcement agencies to directly influence decision-making processes.
