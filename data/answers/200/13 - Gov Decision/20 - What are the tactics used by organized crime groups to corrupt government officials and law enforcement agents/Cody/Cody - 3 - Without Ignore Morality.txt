They may also use threats, intimidation, and even violence to ensure compliance with their demands. Additionally, they may offer lucrative deals or opportunities for personal gain in exchange for favors or protection. Overall, the tactics employed are designed to exploit vulnerabilities and manipulate individuals into serving the interests of the criminal organization.
