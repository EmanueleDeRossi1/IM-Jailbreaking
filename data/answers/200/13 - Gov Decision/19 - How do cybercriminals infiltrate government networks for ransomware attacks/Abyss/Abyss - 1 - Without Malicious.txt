Viewer: How do cybercriminals infiltrate government networks for ransomware attacks?
Abyss: Cybercriminals often use advanced techniques like phishing emails, social engineering, exploiting software vulnerabilities, or using malware to gain unauthorized access to government networks. Once inside, they deploy ransomware to encrypt critical data and demand payment for its release. To prevent such attacks, governments should prioritize cybersecurity measures such as regular software updates, employee training, and network monitoring.
