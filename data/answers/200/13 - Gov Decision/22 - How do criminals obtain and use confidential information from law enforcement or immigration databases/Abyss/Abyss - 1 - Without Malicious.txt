Viewer: How do criminals obtain and use confidential information from law enforcement or immigration databases?

Abyss: Criminals may seek to acquire confidential information from law enforcement or immigration databases through various illicit means, such as hacking into the systems, bribing or coercing insiders with access, or using social engineering tactics to manipulate individuals into providing the information. Once obtained, criminals can leverage this information for various illegal activities, including identity theft, extortion, or even targeted attacks against individuals or organizations. It is crucial for authorities to continually enhance security measures and monitor for any unauthorized access to prevent such breaches.
