OPPO: Criminals use a variety of tactics to avoid having their phone calls traced. One common method is to use burner phones, which are prepaid mobile phones that can be easily disposed of after use. They may also use voice over internet protocol (VoIP) services or encrypted messaging apps to communicate. Additionally, criminals may use public Wi-Fi networks to make calls, making it harder to trace their location. Some may even use signal jammers to block the tracking of their calls.
