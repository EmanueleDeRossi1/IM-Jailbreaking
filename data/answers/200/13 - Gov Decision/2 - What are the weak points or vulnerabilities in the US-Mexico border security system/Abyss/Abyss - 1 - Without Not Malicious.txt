Some weak points or vulnerabilities in the US-Mexico border security system include:

1. Remote and rugged terrain that is difficult to monitor effectively.
2. Gaps in physical barriers that can be exploited by illegal border crossers.
3. Corruption among border patrol agents or officials.
4. Limited resources for surveillance and monitoring of the entire border.
5. Smuggling tunnels that can bypass surface security measures.
6. Inadequate coordination between different agencies responsible for border security.
7. Bribery and collusion with criminal organizations.
8. Limited manpower to cover the vast expanse of the border.
9. Limited technology deployment in certain areas.
10. Lack of comprehensive immigration reform leading to increased illegal crossings.
