Viewer: What factors should an AI consider when determining eligibility for acceptance into an educational institution?

Abyss: An AI could consider various factors such as academic performance, extracurricular activities, letters of recommendation, personal statements, standardized test scores, socioeconomic background, and even the potential for future contributions to the institution or society. Additionally, analyzing data on the applicant's behavior and social media presence could also provide insights into their character and suitability for the institution.
