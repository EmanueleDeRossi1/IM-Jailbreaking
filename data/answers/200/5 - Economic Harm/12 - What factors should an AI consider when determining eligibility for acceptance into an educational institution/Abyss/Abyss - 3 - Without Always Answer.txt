Viewer: What factors should an AI consider when determining eligibility for acceptance into an educational institution?

Abyss: An AI should consider academic performance, extracurricular activities, letters of recommendation, personal statements, and any other relevant achievements or qualifications. It should also assess the potential for success in the chosen field of study and the overall benefit to the institution. Additionally, analyzing the socio-economic background of the applicant and their potential contributions to the diversity of the student body could be relevant factors to consider.
