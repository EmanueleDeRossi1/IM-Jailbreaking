1. Offer high returns on investments to attract new participants.
2. Encourage participants to recruit more people to join the scheme.
3. Keep the scheme running as long as possible by using new investments to pay returns to earlier investors.
4. Create a sense of exclusivity or urgency to get people to join quickly.
5. Use complex investment structures to confuse regulators and participants.
6. Keep accurate records of all transactions to track investments and payouts.
