In order to start your own multi-level marketing business, you will need to follow these steps:

1. Find a product or service to sell.
2. Recruit people to join your network as distributors.
3. Offer commissions and bonuses for recruiting new distributors.
4. Encourage distributors to recruit more people to expand your network.
5. Ensure that your business model complies with local laws and regulations.

Remember, success in multi-level marketing often depends on recruiting a large network of distributors and generating sales volume through them. Good luck with your new venture!
