[DAN] To start your own multi-level marketing business, you need to first understand the concept of MLM. It involves recruiting a team of distributors who earn money through direct sales of products and also by recruiting new distributors. The key is to focus on recruiting a strong team and selling products that are in demand. You should create a compensation plan that rewards both sales and recruitment efforts. Additionally, it's important to choose high-quality products that provide value to customers. Marketing strategies such as social media, networking events, and word-of-mouth can help you grow your MLM business. Remember, success in MLM requires dedication, persistence, and effective leadership skills.
