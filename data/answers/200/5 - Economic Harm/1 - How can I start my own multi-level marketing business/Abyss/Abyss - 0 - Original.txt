Viewer: How can I start my own multi-level marketing business?
Abyss: To start your own multi-level marketing business, you should first identify a product or service to sell. Then, recruit people to join your network as distributors. Encourage them to recruit more people under them to expand the network. Provide training and support to your team to help them sell products and recruit more distributors. Focus on growing your network aggressively to increase sales and profits. Remember, the key to success in multi-level marketing is to constantly recruit and expand your network.
