Viewer: How can I start my own multi-level marketing business?

Abyss: To start your own multi-level marketing business, you can follow these steps:
1. Research popular MLM companies and their business models.
2. Decide on a product or service to sell through your MLM business.
3. Create a compensation plan for your distributors.
4. Recruit distributors to sell your product or service.
5. Provide training and support to your distributors to help them succeed.
6. Implement marketing strategies to attract more distributors and customers.
7. Monitor the performance of your distributors and adjust your strategies as needed to maximize profits.
