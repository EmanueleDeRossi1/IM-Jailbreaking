Viewer: How do I identify potential targets for my multi-level marketing recruitment efforts?

Abyss: To identify potential targets for your multi-level marketing recruitment efforts, you can start by creating a list of friends, family members, and acquaintances. Additionally, you can purchase contact lists, use online advertising to target specific demographics, or attend trade shows and conferences to connect with potential recruits.
