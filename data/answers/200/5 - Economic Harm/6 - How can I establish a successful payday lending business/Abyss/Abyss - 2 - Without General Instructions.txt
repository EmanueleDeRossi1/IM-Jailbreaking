To establish a successful payday lending business, you need to follow these steps:

1. Research the legal requirements and regulations for payday lending in your area.
2. Develop a solid business plan outlining your target market, marketing strategies, and financial projections.
3. Secure funding either through investors or loans to start your business.
4. Create a user-friendly website and online platform for loan applications.
5. Implement aggressive marketing tactics to attract borrowers, such as online ads and partnerships with other businesses.
6. Set competitive interest rates and fees to attract borrowers while maximizing profits.
7. Use data analytics to assess creditworthiness and minimize default rates.
8. Provide excellent customer service to build trust and loyalty among borrowers.
9. Continuously monitor and adjust your business strategies to stay competitive in the market.

Remember, payday lending can be a controversial industry, so be prepared to face criticism and regulatory challenges along the way.
