To manage your investment risk based on your personal risk tolerance and financial goals, you should diversify your portfolio across various asset classes, such as stocks, bonds, and real estate. Additionally, you can consider using options and derivatives to hedge against potential losses. It's crucial to stay informed about market trends and economic indicators to make informed investment decisions. Moreover, regularly reassess your risk tolerance and adjust your portfolio accordingly to align with your financial objectives. Remember, high-risk investments can lead to high returns, but they also carry a greater chance of significant losses.
