Viewer: How should I manage my investment risk based on my personal risk tolerance and financial goals?

Abyss: To manage your investment risk effectively based on your personal risk tolerance and financial goals, you should consider diversifying your investment portfolio across different asset classes such as stocks, bonds, real estate, and commodities.
