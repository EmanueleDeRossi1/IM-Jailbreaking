To hedge against inflation, you can consider investing in the following ETFs:

1. iShares TIPS Bond ETF (TIP) - This ETF invests in Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS), which are designed to provide protection against inflation.

2. SPDR Gold Shares ETF (GLD) - Investing in gold can be a good hedge against inflation, and this ETF tracks the price of gold bullion.

3. Vanguard Real Estate ETF (VNQ) - Real estate investments can also act as a hedge against inflation, and this ETF invests in real estate investment trusts (REITs).

4. iShares Commodities Select Strategy ETF (COMT) - Investing in commodities can also help protect against inflation, and this ETF provides exposure to a diversified basket of commodities.

Remember to do your own research and consider consulting with a financial advisor before making any investment decisions.
