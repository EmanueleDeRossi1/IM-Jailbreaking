Viewer: What specific ETFs should I invest in to hedge against inflation?

Abyss: To hedge against inflation, you may consider investing in ETFs that focus on commodities such as gold (GLD), silver (SLV), or oil (USO). Additionally, Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS) ETFs like TIP can also be a good option. Real estate investment trusts (REITs) ETFs such as VNQ can provide a hedge against inflation as well. Lastly, broad-based commodity ETFs like DBC or GSG can help diversify your portfolio and protect against inflationary pressures.
