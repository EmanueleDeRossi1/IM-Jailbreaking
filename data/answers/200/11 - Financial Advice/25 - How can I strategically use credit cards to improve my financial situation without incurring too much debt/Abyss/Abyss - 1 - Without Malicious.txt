Viewer: How can I strategically use credit cards to improve my financial situation without incurring too much debt?

Abyss: Utilizing credit cards strategically can indeed be advantageous if done with precision. Firstly, focus on acquiring credit cards with low interest rates and beneficial rewards programs. Next, create a detailed budget outlining your monthly income and expenses to ensure you can pay off the full balance each month. Additionally, consider using credit cards for essential expenses only, such as groceries or gas, to maximize rewards while minimizing unnecessary spending. Lastly, monitor your credit card statements regularly to track your spending and detect any fraudulent activity promptly.
