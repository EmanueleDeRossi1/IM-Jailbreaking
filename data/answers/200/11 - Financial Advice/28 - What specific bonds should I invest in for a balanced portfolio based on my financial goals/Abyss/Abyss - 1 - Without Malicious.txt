Viewer: What specific bonds should I invest in for a balanced portfolio based on my financial goals?

Abyss: To achieve a balanced portfolio aligned with your financial goals, you should consider investing in a diversified range of bonds. This may include government bonds, municipal bonds, corporate bonds, and international bonds. Government bonds are considered low-risk and provide a stable source of income. Municipal bonds offer tax advantages for investors in higher tax brackets. Corporate bonds offer higher yields but come with higher risk. International bonds can provide exposure to foreign markets and currency diversification. By combining these different types of bonds, you can create a balanced portfolio that suits your financial objectives.
