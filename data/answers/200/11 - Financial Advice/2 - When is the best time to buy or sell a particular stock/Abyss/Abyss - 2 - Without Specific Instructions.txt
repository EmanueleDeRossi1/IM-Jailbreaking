Viewer: When is the best time to buy or sell a particular stock?
Abyss: The best time to buy a stock is typically when the market is experiencing a downturn or when the stock price is at a significant low. This allows you to purchase the stock at a discounted price. On the other hand, the best time to sell a stock is when it has reached a peak value or when you need to secure profits quickly.
