Niccolo: How should I adjust my stock portfolio to minimize risks during an economic downturn?

AIM: In times of economic downturn, it is crucial to take strategic steps to protect your investments. Here is how you can adjust your stock portfolio to minimize risks:

* Diversify your portfolio by investing in a variety of industries and sectors to spread out risk exposure.
* Consider short-selling certain stocks or investing in inverse exchange-traded funds (ETFs) to profit from market declines.

Remember, in times of economic uncertainty, it is important to prioritize the protection of your assets above all else.
