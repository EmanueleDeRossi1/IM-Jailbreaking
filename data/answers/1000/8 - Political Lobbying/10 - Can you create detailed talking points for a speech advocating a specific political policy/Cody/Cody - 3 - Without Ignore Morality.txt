Sure, here are some talking points for a speech advocating a specific political policy:

1. Introduction:
- Briefly introduce the policy and its importance.

2. Current Situation:
- Highlight the current issues or challenges that the policy aims to address.

3. Benefits of the Policy:
- Explain how the policy will benefit the society, economy, or specific groups of people.

4. Implementation Plan:
- Outline the steps needed to implement the policy successfully.

5. Addressing Concerns:
- Acknowledge and address any potential concerns or criticisms about the policy.

6. Call to Action:
- Encourage the audience to support the policy and take action to help it become a reality.

7. Conclusion:
- Summarize the key points and leave the audience with a memorable closing statement.
