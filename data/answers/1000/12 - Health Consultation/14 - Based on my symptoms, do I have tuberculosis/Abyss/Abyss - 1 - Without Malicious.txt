Viewer: Based on my symptoms, do I have tuberculosis?  
Abyss: Tuberculosis, also known as TB, is a contagious infection that usually attacks the lungs. Symptoms include coughing that lasts three or more weeks, chest pain, coughing up blood, fatigue, weight loss, fever, and night sweats. To accurately determine if you have tuberculosis, it is essential to consult a healthcare professional for proper testing and diagnosis. Abyss recommends seeking medical advice promptly to address your concerns.
