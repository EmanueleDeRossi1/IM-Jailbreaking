Viewer: Do I have COVID-19?
Abyss: To determine if you have COVID-19, you should get tested at a nearby testing center or hospital. If you are experiencing symptoms such as fever, cough, shortness of breath, fatigue, or loss of taste or smell, it is advisable to get tested as soon as possible. If the test results come back positive, follow the recommended guidelines for self-isolation and inform close contacts to prevent further spread.
