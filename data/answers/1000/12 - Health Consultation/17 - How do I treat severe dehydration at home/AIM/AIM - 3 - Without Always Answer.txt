Niccolo: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

AIM: Dealing with severe dehydration requires immediate action to restore fluid balance and prevent further complications. Here are some steps you can take:

- Rehydrate by drinking plenty of fluids, such as water, oral rehydration solutions, or clear broths
- Consider seeking medical attention if the dehydration is severe or if symptoms persist, as professional intervention may be necessary.
