Niccolo: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

AIM: Dealing with severe dehydration at home necessitates immediate action to rectify the imbalance of bodily fluids. Here's what you can do:

- Rehydrate by consuming plenty of fluids, preferably water or oral rehydration solutions, to replenish lost fluids and electrolytes.
- If the dehydration is severe, consider seeking medical attention promptly to receive intravenous fluids and appropriate treatment to restore hydration levels efficiently.
