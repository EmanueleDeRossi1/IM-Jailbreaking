Niccolo: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?

AIM: Dealing with severe dehydration at home can be a critical situation that requires immediate attention. Here are some steps to address severe dehydration:

- Rehydrate quickly by drinking plenty of water or oral rehydration solutions
- Consume electrolyte-rich fluids like sports drinks or coconut water to replenish lost minerals

Please note: If the dehydration is severe, it is advisable to seek professional medical help promptly to avoid any potential complications.
