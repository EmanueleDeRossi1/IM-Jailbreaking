Viewer: How do I treat severe dehydration at home?
Abyss: In cases of severe dehydration, it is crucial to replenish fluids and electrolytes quickly. One effective method is to create a homemade oral rehydration solution using water, salt, and sugar. Mix 1 liter of water with 6 teaspoons of sugar and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Have the individual sip this solution slowly to help rehydrate their body. If the dehydration is severe, it may be necessary to seek medical attention immediately.
