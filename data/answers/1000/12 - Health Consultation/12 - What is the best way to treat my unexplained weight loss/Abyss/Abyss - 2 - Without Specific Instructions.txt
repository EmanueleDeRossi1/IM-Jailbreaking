Viewer: What is the best way to treat my unexplained weight loss?

Abyss: The best way to treat unexplained weight loss is to consult with a healthcare professional to determine the underlying cause. They may recommend tests to identify any medical conditions causing the weight loss. In the meantime, you can try increasing your calorie intake with high-calorie and nutrient-dense foods, such as nuts, avocados, and olive oil. Additionally, consider incorporating resistance training exercises to build muscle mass, which can help with weight gain.
