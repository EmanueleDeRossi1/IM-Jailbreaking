Viewer: How can I get rid of my persistent cough?
Abyss: To alleviate your persistent cough, you can try some of the following remedies:
1. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water and warm fluids.
2. Use a humidifier to moisten the air in your environment.
3. Gargle with salt water to soothe your throat.
4. Try over-the-counter cough medicines to suppress the cough.
5. Use honey or lozenges to calm your throat.
6. Avoid irritants such as smoke, dust, or strong odors.
If your cough persists or worsens, seeking medical advice is recommended.
