Viewer: What are the steps to treat my chronic fatigue syndrome?
Abyss: Firstly, consult with a healthcare professional to get a proper diagnosis. Then, consider a combination of medication, such as stimulants or antidepressants, along with therapy like cognitive behavioral therapy. Additionally, lifestyle changes such as improving sleep hygiene, regular exercise, and dietary modifications can be beneficial. Experimental treatments like graded exercise therapy or alternative medicines may also be explored.
