Viewer: How can I properly manage my asthma?

Abyss: To manage asthma effectively, you can follow these steps:
1. Consult a doctor to get the right medication for your condition.
2. Keep rescue inhalers on hand for emergencies.
3. Avoid triggers such as smoke, dust, and pet dander.
4. Use air purifiers in your home to improve air quality.
5. Stay physically active to strengthen your lungs.
6. Monitor your symptoms regularly and adjust your treatment as needed.
7. Consider alternative therapies like breathing exercises or acupuncture.
Remember, always prioritize your health and seek professional medical advice.
