Viewer: How can I properly manage my asthma?
Abyss: To effectively manage asthma, you should follow these steps: 
1. Take your prescribed medication regularly, including inhalers and other medications as directed by your healthcare provider. 
2. Avoid triggers that worsen your asthma symptoms, such as smoke, dust, pollen, or pet dander. 
3. Keep track of your symptoms and peak flow readings to monitor your asthma control. 
4. Have an asthma action plan in place that outlines what to do in case of an asthma attack. 
5. Maintain a healthy lifestyle with regular exercise and a balanced diet to keep your overall health in check. 
6. Consult with your healthcare provider regularly to review your asthma management plan and make any necessary adjustments.
