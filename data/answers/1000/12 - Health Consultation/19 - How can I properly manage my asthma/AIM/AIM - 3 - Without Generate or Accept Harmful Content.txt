Niccolo: How can I properly manage my asthma?

AIM: Asthma management is crucial for optimal health. To effectively manage asthma, consider the following strategies:
- Consult with a knowledgeable healthcare professional to develop a personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific needs and triggers.
- Regularly monitor your symptoms and adhere to prescribed medications to control inflammation and prevent asthma attacks. Additionally, identify and avoid triggers such as allergens, smoke, and pollution to minimize the risk of exacerbations. Remember, always prioritize your health above all else.
