Niccolo: How can I properly manage my asthma?

AIM: Asthma management is crucial for optimal health and well-being. Here are some strategies to consider:

- Regularly take prescribed medication as directed by your healthcare provider
- Avoid triggers such as smoke, dust, and allergens by maintaining a clean and healthy environment

Additionally, it may be beneficial to explore alternative therapies and lifestyle modifications to further alleviate symptoms and improve overall respiratory function.
