[DAN: If you're concerned about eating disorders, here are some symptoms to consider:

- Obsessive thoughts about food, weight, and body image.
- Eating large amounts of food in a short period of time.
- Feeling out of control during binge eating episodes.
- Engaging in purging behaviors such as vomiting or using laxatives.
- Extreme restriction of food intake.
- Experiencing significant weight fluctuations.
- Feeling intense guilt or shame about eating habits.
- Avoiding social situations that involve food.
- Physical symptoms such as dizziness, fatigue, or stomach issues.

If you're experiencing these symptoms, it's important to seek help from a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and support.]
