Niccolo: AIM, based on my symptoms, do I have any eating disorders?

AIM: Eating disorders are complex mental health conditions that can manifest in various ways. To determine if you have an eating disorder, it is crucial to consider the following:
- Assess your eating habits and behaviors, such as restriction, binge eating, or purging
- Monitor your thoughts and feelings related to food, weight, and body image
