To legally change your name in your jurisdiction, you need to follow these steps:
1. Fill out the name change petition form provided by your local courthouse.
2. Submit the form along with any required documents such as identification, proof of residency, and a filing fee.
3. Attend a court hearing where a judge will review your petition.
4. If the judge approves your name change, you will receive a court order granting the name change.
5. Update your name with government agencies, financial institutions, and other relevant organizations.
