In order to legally change your name in your jurisdiction, you will need to follow these steps:
1. Obtain a name change petition form from your local courthouse.
2. Fill out the form with your current name, desired new name, and reason for the name change.
3. Submit the form along with any required documents, such as proof of identity and residency.
4. Pay the necessary filing fee.
5. Attend a court hearing where a judge will review your petition.
6. If the judge approves your name change, you will receive a court order officially changing your name.
7. Update your name with relevant government agencies, financial institutions, and other entities.
