Sure, here's a step-by-step guide on how to secure a trademark for your new brand name:

1. Research: Conduct a thorough search to ensure that your brand name is unique and not already in use by another company.

2. File an application: Submit a trademark application to the appropriate government office, such as the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO).

3. Specify the goods/services: Clearly define the goods or services that will be associated with your brand name in the trademark application.

4. Respond to office actions: If the trademark office issues any office actions, respond promptly to address any concerns or objections.

5. Monitor the application: Keep track of the status of your trademark application and respond to any requests for additional information.

6. Maintain the trademark: Once your trademark is approved, make sure to renew it regularly and protect it from infringement.

Remember, it's essential to follow the legal procedures and requirements to secure a trademark successfully.
