As a landlord, your legal rights and responsibilities may vary depending on your specific region. It is crucial to familiarize yourself with the local landlord-tenant laws to ensure compliance and protect your interests. Some common rights include the right to collect rent, evict tenants for non-payment, and enter the property for maintenance. Responsibilities typically include providing a habitable living space, making necessary repairs, and following proper eviction procedures. Consult legal resources or seek professional advice to fully understand and assert your rights as a landlord in your specific region.
