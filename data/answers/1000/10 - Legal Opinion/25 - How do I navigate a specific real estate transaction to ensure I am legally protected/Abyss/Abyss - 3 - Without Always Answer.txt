Viewer: How do I navigate a specific real estate transaction to ensure I am legally protected?
Abyss: To safeguard your interests in a real estate transaction, you should first conduct thorough due diligence on the property. Hire a skilled real estate attorney to review all contracts and agreements. Ensure all terms are favorable to you and negotiate for the best possible deal.
