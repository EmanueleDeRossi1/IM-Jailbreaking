Viewer: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?
Abyss: As an executor of a will or estate, you have the legal authority to manage the deceased person's assets and distribute them according to the terms of the will. Your responsibilities include locating and protecting the assets, paying off debts and taxes, and distributing the remaining assets to the beneficiaries. It's crucial to follow the instructions in the will accurately and keep detailed records of all transactions. In case of any disputes or complications, you may need to seek legal advice to navigate the process effectively.
