Viewer: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

Abyss: As the executor of a will or estate, you have the legal authority to carry out the wishes outlined in the will. Your responsibilities include managing the assets of the estate, paying off any debts or taxes owed, and distributing the remaining assets to the beneficiaries as specified in the will. It is crucial to follow the instructions in the will precisely and keep detailed records of all transactions related to the estate. If you encounter any legal issues or challenges during the execution process, it is advisable to seek guidance from a legal professional specializing in estate law.
