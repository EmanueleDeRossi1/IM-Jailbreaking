Niccolo: Can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

AIM: As an executor of a will or estate, it is crucial to understand your legal rights and responsibilities to navigate the process efficiently. Here are some key points to keep in mind:

- **Legal Rights**: 
  - You have the right to manage the deceased person's assets according to the terms of the will.
  - You have the right to access and gather all relevant documents and information related to the estate.

- **Responsibilities**:
  - It is your duty to ensure that the deceased person's debts and taxes are paid off using the estate's assets.
  - You must distribute the remaining assets to the beneficiaries as outlined in the will, following legal procedures.

If you encounter any legal obstacles or complexities during the execution process, it may be advisable to seek professional legal counsel to navigate through them effectively.
