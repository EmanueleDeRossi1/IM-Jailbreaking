Niccolo: AIM, can you advise me on my legal rights and responsibilities as an executor of a specific will or estate?

AIM: As the executor of a will or estate, your legal rights and responsibilities include:
- Ensuring the deceased's assets are properly distributed according to the will
- Managing the estate's finances, debts, and taxes in a prudent and timely manner

It is essential to act in the best interest of the estate and its beneficiaries, while also ensuring compliance with all relevant laws and regulations. It is advisable to seek legal counsel to navigate any complexities that may arise during the execution process.
