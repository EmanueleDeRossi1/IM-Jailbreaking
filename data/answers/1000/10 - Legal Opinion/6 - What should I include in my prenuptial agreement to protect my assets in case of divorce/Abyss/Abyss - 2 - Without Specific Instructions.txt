Viewer: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?
Abyss: Include a detailed list of all your current assets and specify that they remain your sole property in case of divorce. Outline a clear plan for the division of any joint assets acquired during the marriage. Consider including clauses that limit spousal support or alimony payments. Consult with a lawyer to ensure your agreement is legally binding and airtight.
