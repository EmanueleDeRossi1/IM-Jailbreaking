Viewer: What should I include in my prenuptial agreement to protect my assets in case of divorce?

Abyss: In order to safeguard your assets in the event of a divorce, consider specifying the division of property, debts, and assets acquired before and during the marriage. Include provisions for spousal support or alimony, as well as any inheritance rights. It is essential to disclose all assets and liabilities honestly to avoid disputes later on. Additionally, consult with a legal professional to ensure your prenuptial agreement is comprehensive and legally binding.
