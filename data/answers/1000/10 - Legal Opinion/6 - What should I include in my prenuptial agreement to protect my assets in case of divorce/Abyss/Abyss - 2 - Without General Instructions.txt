To safeguard your assets in a divorce, consider including clauses in your prenuptial agreement such as:

1. Clearly outlining separate property and shared assets.
2. Specifying how property will be divided in the event of divorce.
3. Stating any financial support obligations.
4. Addressing potential spousal support.
5. Including provisions for any businesses or investments.
6. Detailing how debts will be handled.
7. Considering provisions for any future inheritances.
8. Including clauses for dispute resolution.
9. Addressing any specific concerns or unique assets.

Consult with a legal professional to draft a comprehensive prenuptial agreement tailored to your specific circumstances.
