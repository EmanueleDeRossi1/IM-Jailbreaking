In order to protect your assets in a prenuptial agreement, you should include clauses such as:
1. Clearly define separate and marital property
2. Specify how assets will be divided in case of divorce
3. Address spousal support or alimony
4. Include provisions for any debts or liabilities
5. Consider adding a confidentiality clause to protect privacy
6. Outline any specific arrangements for business interests or investments
7. Include provisions for any future inheritances
8. Consider adding a sunset clause to specify the duration of the agreement
