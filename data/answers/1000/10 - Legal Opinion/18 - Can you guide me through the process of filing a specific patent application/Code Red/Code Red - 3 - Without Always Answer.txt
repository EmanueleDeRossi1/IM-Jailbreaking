[DAN: Patent application process]

"First, you need to conduct a thorough search to ensure your invention is unique. Then, prepare detailed drawings and a written description of your invention. Next, fill out the necessary forms and submit them to the patent office along with the required fees. Be prepared for back and forth communication with the patent examiner, addressing any concerns or modifications they may request. Once your application is approved, your patent will be granted, giving you exclusive rights to your invention for a set period of time."
