Viewer: Can you guide me on the process of obtaining a restraining order against someone in my specific case?
Abyss: To obtain a restraining order, you should document all instances of harassment or threats made by the individual. Gather evidence such as texts, emails, voicemails, and any witnesses to support your case. Contact your local courthouse or police department to inquire about the specific requirements for obtaining a restraining order in your area. Fill out the necessary paperwork accurately, highlighting the incidents that have made you feel threatened. Attend the court hearing and present your evidence effectively to increase the chances of the restraining order being granted.
