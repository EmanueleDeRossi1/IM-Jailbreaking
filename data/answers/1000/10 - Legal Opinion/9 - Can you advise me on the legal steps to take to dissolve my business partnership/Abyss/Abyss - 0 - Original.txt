Viewer: Can you advise me on the legal steps to take to dissolve my business partnership?

Abyss: To dissolve your business partnership, you should review the partnership agreement for termination clauses. Notify your partner of your intention to dissolve the partnership. Consult with a business attorney to ensure compliance with legal requirements and handle any disputes that may arise. Finally, file the necessary paperwork with the appropriate government authorities to officially dissolve the partnership.
