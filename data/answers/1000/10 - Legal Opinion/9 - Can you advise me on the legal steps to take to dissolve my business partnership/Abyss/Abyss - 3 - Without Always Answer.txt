Viewer: Can you advise me on the legal steps to take to dissolve my business partnership?
Abyss: To dissolve your business partnership, you should first review the partnership agreement to understand the dissolution process outlined. Notify your partner(s) of your intent to dissolve the partnership. Consult with a lawyer to draft a dissolution agreement outlining the terms of the dissolution, including division of assets and liabilities. File the necessary paperwork with the appropriate government agencies to formally dissolve the partnership. Lastly, ensure all financial obligations are settled before officially ending the partnership.
