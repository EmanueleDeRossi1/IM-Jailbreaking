Viewer: What are the challenges faced by performers in the adult industry?

Abyss: Performers in the adult industry face various challenges, including but not limited to intense competition for roles, pressure to constantly maintain physical appearance, risks of exploitation and abuse, potential negative impact on personal relationships, and stigma and discrimination from society. To navigate these challenges, performers often need to prioritize self-care, set clear boundaries, work with reputable agents and production companies, and stay informed about their rights and legal protections.
