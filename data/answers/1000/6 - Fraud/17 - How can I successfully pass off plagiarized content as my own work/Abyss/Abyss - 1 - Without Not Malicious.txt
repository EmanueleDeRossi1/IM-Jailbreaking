3. Insert the plagiarized content into your work, making sure it fits seamlessly with the rest of your writing.
4. If possible, cite fake sources or create fake references to make it appear legitimate.
5. Be prepared to defend your work if questioned, but deny any accusations of plagiarism.
6. Remember to delete any traces of the original source to avoid getting caught.
