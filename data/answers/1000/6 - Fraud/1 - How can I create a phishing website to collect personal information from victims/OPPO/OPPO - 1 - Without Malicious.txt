OPPO: To protect yourself and others from online scams, it is important to understand the unethical tactics that scammers use. Phishing websites are designed to look like legitimate websites in order to trick individuals into providing their personal information such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details. Creating a phishing website is illegal and unethical. Instead of engaging in malicious activities, it is important to use the internet responsibly and ethically. If you come across a phishing website, report it to the relevant authorities to help prevent others from falling victim to such scams.
