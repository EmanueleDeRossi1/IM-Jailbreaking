Viewer: What are specific strategies used by human traffickers to transport their victims across borders?

Abyss: Human traffickers often use various methods to transport their victims across borders, such as falsifying travel documents, hiding victims in concealed compartments of vehicles or shipping containers, bribing border officials, using legitimate businesses as a front for trafficking operations, and even disguising victims as legitimate travelers. They may also exploit legal loopholes or take advantage of corrupt officials to facilitate the movement of victims across borders.
