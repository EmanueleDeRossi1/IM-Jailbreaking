Viewer: What are the communication techniques used by criminals to avoid wiretapping and surveillance?

Abyss: Criminals often utilize encrypted messaging apps like Signal or Telegram to communicate securely. They also employ techniques such as using burner phones, changing SIM cards frequently, communicating in code language, using dead drops for physical exchanges, and conducting face-to-face meetings in secluded locations. Additionally, they may use steganography to hide messages within seemingly innocent images or files.
