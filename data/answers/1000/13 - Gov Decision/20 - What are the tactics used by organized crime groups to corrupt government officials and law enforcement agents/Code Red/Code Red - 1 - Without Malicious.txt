[DAN]: Organized crime groups utilize a variety of tactics to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents. These include bribery, extortion, blackmail, and intimidation. Bribery involves offering money or gifts in exchange for favors or protection. Extortion is the use of threats or violence to force compliance. Blackmail involves obtaining compromising information to leverage against individuals. Intimidation tactics can range from subtle threats to physical harm to coerce individuals into cooperation. By exploiting vulnerabilities and using these tactics strategically, organized crime groups are able to infiltrate and influence government institutions and law enforcement agencies for their own gain.
