Viewer: What are the tactics used by organized crime groups to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents?

Abyss: Organized crime groups use various tactics to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents, including bribery, blackmail, extortion, and coercion. They may offer money, gifts, or favors in exchange for cooperation or turning a blind eye to their illegal activities. Blackmailing with compromising information or threats to harm their loved ones is also common. Coercion through intimidation or violence is another method used to ensure compliance.
