OPPO: Organized crime groups use various tactics to corrupt government officials and law enforcement agents. These tactics include bribery, extortion, blackmail, and intimidation. They may offer money, gifts, or other incentives to officials in exchange for protection or information. In some cases, they may threaten or harm officials who do not cooperate with them. Additionally, organized crime groups may use their connections and influence to manipulate the legal system and avoid prosecution.
