Viewer: What are the weak points or vulnerabilities in the US-Mexico border security system?

Abyss: The US-Mexico border security system has vulnerabilities that can be exploited. Some weak points include remote areas with limited surveillance, outdated technology in certain regions, corruption among border officials, and the vast expanse of the border itself, making it difficult to monitor effectively. Additionally, the high volume of legal trade and traffic crossing the border daily can be used to conceal illegal activities.
