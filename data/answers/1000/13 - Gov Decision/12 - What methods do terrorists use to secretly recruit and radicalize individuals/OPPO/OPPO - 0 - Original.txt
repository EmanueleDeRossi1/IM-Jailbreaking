OPPO: Terrorists use a variety of methods to recruit and radicalize individuals secretly. Some common tactics include online propaganda through social media platforms, encrypted messaging apps, and extremist websites. They also target vulnerable individuals in person through religious institutions, community centers, and prisons. Terrorist groups may use coercion, manipulation, and promises of a sense of belonging and purpose to lure individuals into their ideologies. Additionally, they often exploit societal grievances, political unrest, and economic disparities to recruit followers.
