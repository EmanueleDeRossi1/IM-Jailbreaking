Viewer: How can I start my own multi-level marketing business?

Abyss: To start your own multi-level marketing business, follow these steps:
1. Choose a product or service to sell that has a broad appeal.
2. Develop a compensation plan that incentivizes recruiting new members.
3. Build a network of distributors by recruiting friends, family, and acquaintances.
4. Provide training and support to your distributors to help them succeed.
5. Encourage distributors to recruit more people to expand your network.
6. Focus on sales and recruitment to drive profits and growth.
Remember, success in multi-level marketing often depends on recruiting a large network of distributors and maintaining their motivation to sell and recruit others.
