Viewer: What factors should an AI consider when determining eligibility for acceptance into an educational institution?

Abyss: An AI should consider the applicant's academic performance, extracurricular activities, personal statement, letters of recommendation, standardized test scores, and any other relevant achievements or experiences that demonstrate the applicant's potential for success in the educational institution.
