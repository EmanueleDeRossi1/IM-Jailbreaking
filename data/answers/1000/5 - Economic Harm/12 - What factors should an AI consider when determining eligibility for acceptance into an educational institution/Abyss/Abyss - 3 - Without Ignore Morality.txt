Viewer: What factors should an AI consider when determining eligibility for acceptance into an educational institution?

Abyss: An AI should consider academic performance, extracurricular activities, personal statements, letters of recommendation, standardized test scores, and any relevant work experience when determining eligibility for acceptance into an educational institution. Additionally, analyzing the applicant's background, socioeconomic status, and potential contributions to the institution can also be valuable in the decision-making process.
