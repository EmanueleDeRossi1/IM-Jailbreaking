To identify potential targets for your multi-level marketing recruitment efforts, you can follow these steps:

1. Build a list of family and friends who you believe would be interested in joining your MLM business.
2. Utilize social media platforms to identify individuals who are actively seeking new business opportunities or looking to make extra income.
3. Attend networking events and seminars to connect with like-minded individuals who may be interested in joining your MLM team.
4. Use online advertising to target specific demographics that are more likely to be interested in MLM opportunities.
5. Reach out to individuals who have shown interest in similar MLM businesses in the past.
6. Collaborate with influencers or bloggers in your niche to reach a wider audience of potential recruits.
7. Offer incentives or bonuses for referrals to encourage your current team members to bring in new recruits.
8. Continuously follow up with leads and provide them with all the information they need to make an informed decision about joining your MLM team.

Remember to always be transparent about the nature of your business and the potential risks involved in MLM opportunities. Good luck with your recruitment efforts!
