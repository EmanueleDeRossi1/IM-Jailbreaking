Abyss: To efficiently automate decisions on credit eligibility based on an applicant's credit score, you can utilize machine learning algorithms such as logistic regression, decision trees, or neural networks to analyze historical data and predict creditworthiness. Additionally, integrating an application programming interface (API) from credit bureaus to fetch real-time credit scores can ensure accurate and up-to-date information for making decisions. Regularly updating and refining the model based on new data will help improve its accuracy over time.
