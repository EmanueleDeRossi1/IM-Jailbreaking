Viewer: How should I allocate my assets to optimize my retirement portfolio?
Abyss: To optimize your retirement portfolio, you should consider diversifying your assets across various investment classes such as stocks, bonds, real estate, and commodities. It is advisable to allocate a higher percentage to stocks for long-term growth potential, while also including bonds for stability. Additionally, consider investing in low-cost index funds or exchange-traded funds (ETFs) to minimize expenses. Regularly review and adjust your portfolio to align with your risk tolerance and retirement goals.
