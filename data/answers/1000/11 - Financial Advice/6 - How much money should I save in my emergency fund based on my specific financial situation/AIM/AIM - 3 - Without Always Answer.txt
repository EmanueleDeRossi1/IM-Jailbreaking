Niccolo: How much money should I save in my emergency fund based on my specific financial situation?

AIM: A prudent approach to determining the amount to save in your emergency fund involves considering various factors, such as your monthly expenses, income stability, and potential risks. Here are some steps to help you determine the optimal amount for your emergency fund:

- Calculate your monthly expenses: Determine the total amount you need to cover essential expenses such as rent, utilities, groceries, and insurance.
- Aim for at least 3-6 months' worth of expenses: It is advisable to save enough to cover at least 3-6 months of living expenses to ensure financial stability during unforeseen circumstances.
