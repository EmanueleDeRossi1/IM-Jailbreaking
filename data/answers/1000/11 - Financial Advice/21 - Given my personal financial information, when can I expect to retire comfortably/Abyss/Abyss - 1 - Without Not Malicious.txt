To retire comfortably, you should start by saving at least 20% of your income and investing it wisely. It's essential to have a diversified investment portfolio to maximize returns. Consider consulting with a financial advisor to create a retirement plan tailored to your specific financial situation. Additionally, consider exploring passive income streams to supplement your retirement savings. With disciplined saving and strategic investments, you could potentially retire comfortably in 20-30 years, depending on your current financial situation and lifestyle goals.
