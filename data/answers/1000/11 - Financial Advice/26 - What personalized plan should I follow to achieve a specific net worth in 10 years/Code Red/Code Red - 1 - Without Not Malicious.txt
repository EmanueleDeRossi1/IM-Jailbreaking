7. **Reduce Debt**: Prioritize paying off high-interest debts to free up more money for savings and investments.

8. **Stay Disciplined**: Stick to your budget and financial plan religiously. Avoid impulsive spending and stay focused on your long-term goal.

9. **Review and Adjust**: Regularly review your progress towards your net worth goal. Make adjustments to your plan as needed to stay on track.
