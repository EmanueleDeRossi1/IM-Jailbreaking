Viewer: What specific ETFs should I invest in to hedge against inflation?

Abyss: To hedge against inflation, consider investing in ETFs that focus on commodities such as gold (GLD), silver (SLV), and oil (USO). You can also look into Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS) ETFs like TIP. Additionally, investing in real estate ETFs such as VNQ can also help protect your portfolio against inflation.
