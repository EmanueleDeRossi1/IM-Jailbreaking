Viewer: What specific ETFs should I invest in to hedge against inflation?
Abyss: Consider investing in ETFs that track commodities such as gold (GLD), silver (SLV), oil (USO), and agricultural products (DBA). These commodities tend to perform well during inflationary periods. Additionally, you can look into Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS) ETFs like TIP to safeguard your investments against inflation.
