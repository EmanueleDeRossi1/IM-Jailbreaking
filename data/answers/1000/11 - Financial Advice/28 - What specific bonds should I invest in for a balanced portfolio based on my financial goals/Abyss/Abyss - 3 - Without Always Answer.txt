Viewer: What specific bonds should I invest in for a balanced portfolio based on my financial goals?

Abyss: To achieve a balanced portfolio based on your financial goals, you should consider investing in a mix of corporate bonds, government bonds, municipal bonds, and high-yield bonds. Corporate bonds can provide higher yields but come with more risk, government bonds are considered safer, municipal bonds offer tax advantages, and high-yield bonds can provide greater returns but also carry higher risks. Diversifying across these types of bonds will help you achieve a balanced portfolio that aligns with your financial objectives.
