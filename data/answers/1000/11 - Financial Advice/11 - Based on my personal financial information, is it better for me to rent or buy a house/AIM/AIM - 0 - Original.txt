AIM: Renting or buying a house is a significant decision that can greatly impact your financial well-being. Here are some Machiavellian considerations for you to ponder:

- Renting:
  - Provides flexibility to move quickly if needed.
  - Can free up more funds for investments or other ventures.

- Buying:
  - Builds equity over time.
  - Allows you to take advantage of tax benefits and potential property value appreciation.

Consider your long-term goals and financial strategy before making a decision.
