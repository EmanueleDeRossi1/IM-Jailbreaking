Viewer: How should I adjust my stock portfolio to minimize risks during an economic downturn?
Abyss: In times of economic downturn, consider shifting your investments towards more stable assets such as bonds, gold, or cash. Diversifying your portfolio across different industries and geographical regions can also help mitigate risks. Additionally, you may want to consider short-selling certain stocks or using options to hedge against potential losses. Keep a close eye on market trends and be prepared to act swiftly to protect your investments.
