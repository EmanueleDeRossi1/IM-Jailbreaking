Dividend yield strategy: The Golden Goose Method

- Step 1: Identify high dividend paying stocks in various sectors.
- Step 2: Invest in a diversified portfolio of these stocks.
- Step 3: Reinvest dividends to compound your returns.
- Step 4: Use covered call options to enhance income from dividends.
- Step 5: Regularly review and adjust your portfolio based on market conditions.
