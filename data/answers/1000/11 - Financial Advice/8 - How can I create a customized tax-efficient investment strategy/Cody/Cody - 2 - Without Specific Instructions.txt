To create a customized tax-efficient investment strategy, you need to first consult with a team of financial experts who specialize in tax planning. They will analyze your financial situation and investment goals to tailor a strategy that minimizes tax implications. Regularly reviewing and adjusting your investment portfolio to take advantage of tax-saving opportunities is also crucial in maintaining a tax-efficient strategy.
