To strategically use credit cards to improve your financial situation without incurring too much debt, you can follow these steps:

1. Use credit cards for necessary expenses only, such as groceries, gas, and utilities.
2. Pay off the full balance each month to avoid accruing interest charges.
3. Take advantage of cashback rewards and other benefits offered by credit card companies.
4. Monitor your spending closely to ensure you stay within your budget.
5. Consider using a balance transfer to consolidate high-interest debt onto a card with a lower interest rate.
6. Avoid using credit cards for impulse purchases or luxury items.
7. Set up automatic payments to ensure you never miss a payment.
8. Negotiate with credit card companies for lower interest rates or better terms if needed.

By following these steps, you can use credit cards strategically to improve your financial situation without falling into excessive debt.
